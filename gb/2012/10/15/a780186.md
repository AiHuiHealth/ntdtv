<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】慰安活证人去世 起诉日本中共袖手</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2012年10月15日讯】10月12号，山西省太原市阳曲县郑家寨村一名90岁高龄老人尹玉林去世。这位老人抱憾而终，因为日本侵华期间她被日军抓去充当<ahref="https://github.com/vtmwhj347/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/慰安妇.md#1">慰安妇</a>，1992年开始，她和其他十五名受害女性站出来起诉日本政府，指控日军禽兽罪行。但是日本法院宣判不予赔偿。<ahref="https://github.com/vtmwhj347/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a>政府对自己的国民讨还公道的努力始终袖手旁观。</p>
<p>1942年春天，日本官兵闯入山西盂县尹玉林家，把她和姐姐一同抓走。日军对姐妹二人进行了轮奸。打那之后，日军时常把姐妹二人和其他被抓来的妇女用刺刀指著，威逼到炮楼，这样持续了一年多的时间。</p>
<p>被称为“中国民间<ahref="https://github.com/vtmwhj347/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/慰安妇.md#1">慰安妇</a>调查第一人”的张双兵告诉《新唐人》，他调查了中国100多位慰安妇。而从1992年开始，十几年时间先后有三批16位女性受害者，到日本向法院起诉日本政府。前后经历了三级法院审理，到2007年的时候宣判。</p>
<p>山西省教师 张双兵：“但是最终日本法庭的判决，承认（慰安妇）这是事实，但是不予赔偿。这种判决我们认为不是不当判决，而是一种野蛮判决。说服不了受害者和家属，也说服不了受苦受难的人民。”</p>
<p>日本法院以时效已过，和个人不得起诉政府为由，判决不予赔偿。</p>
<p>张双兵：“关于时效问题，因为我们战后，中国和日本一直是冷战阶段，就没有建立外交关系，也就没有这个条件提出起诉。再一个，个人不得起诉国家政府这个理由，我们认为，因为是你日本政府下命令侵略中国，为什么你可以欺负人民，但是人民不允许起诉你日本政府？” </p>
<p>两个日本民间组织——“日本山西查明会”和“日本图片展委员会”给尹玉林发来唁电说：“ 大娘的心愿‘得到日本政府的正式谢罪和赔偿，争取恢复名誉’在生前未能实现，我们感到十分愧疚。” </p>
<p>而在尹玉林去世后，山西的慰安妇“活证人”仅剩下6位。</p>
<p>在慰安妇寻求日本政府赔偿问题上，<ahref="https://github.com/vtmwhj347/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a>政府始终保持沉默，更没有出面向日本政府提出交涉。“中国民间保钓联合会”成员冯景华说，他无法理解中共政府的做法。</p>
<p>保钓人士冯景华：“我们国家为什么不做这些事情，我们也很纳闷，我们也很郁闷，我们也希望国家有关部门把这个事情重视起来。但是因为受害者的牵扯面是非常广的。这个事情一旦提到桌面上来以后，像劳工问题，化学武器问题，慰安妇问题，那么有人可能就会认为破坏中日关系，破坏中日友好啦。”</p>
<p>冯景华说，他们保钓人士每年都会去探望这些老人，但是人数一年比一年少。在他们看来，中共政府和日本政府都希望这些人尽快走掉。大家都把这个当成一个包袱。尤其是中共政府。故意视而不见，看着他们这样默默死去。对他们的生活救济和医疗救济，完全没有。</p>
<p>冯景华：“我们在声讨日本人的时候，我们更应该反思我们中国人自己。为什么我们中国的受害者会生活的这么凄惨？为什么日本的加害者他们生活的那么好？我也见过日本兵，包括当年广岛原子弹的受害者，日本政府把他们照顾的很好。但是反过来我们中国呢？这些老人没有医疗保险，也没有其他保险。这也是我们中国社会现状导致的。”</p>
<p>相比之下，韩国政府10月1号在联合国大会主旨演讲中首次提出了慰安妇问题。 8月30号，韩国宪法裁判所对原慰安妇起诉政府的索赔请求案作出判决，认定政府在处理原慰安妇赔偿问题上，没有很认真的与日本政府交涉，违反宪法相关规定。随后，韩国政府要求日本政府就慰安妇的赔偿请求权问题，开展两国政府间的协议。</p>
<p>采访撰稿/秦雪 后制/葛雷</p>
<p>The comfort women witnesses died, prosecuting the Japanese<br />government with the Communists standing by</p>
<p>On Oct. 12th a 90-year-old woman Yin Yulin, from Zheng<br />jia Zhai, Yangqu County, Taiyuan city, Shanxi Province, died.<br />The old woman&#8217;s death was regrettable as she was arrested by the<br />Japanese army to act as comfort women during Japan invading China.<br />From 1992, she and other fifteen female victims come forward<br />to prosecute the Japanese government.<br />They accused the Japanese army of bestial crimes.<br />However, the Japanese court gave no compensation.<br />The Chinese Communist Party (CCP) stood by as its own<br />Citizens made efforts to pursue justice.</p>
<p>In the spring of 1942, the Japanese officers and soldiers broke<br />into Yin Yulin&#8217;s home in the Yu country, Shanxi provinces.<br />They arrested her and her sister together.<br />The two sisters were gang-raped by the Japanese soldiers.<br />The two sisters and other women who were caught were often<br />forced with bayonets to the towers to act as comfort women.<br />This lasted more than a year.</p>
<p>Known as &#8220;the first person of Chinese Folk comfort women<br />investigation” Zhang Shuangbing told NTDTV that he had surveyed more than 100 Chinese comfort women.<br />From 1992, in 10 years, there were three batches of 16 female<br />victims prosecuting the Japanese government in Japanese courts.<br />Through three courts, the case was sentenced in 2007.</p>
<p>Teacher in Shanxi Province, Zhang Shuangbing: “In the end,<br />the Japanese court&#8217;s decision admitted that the comfort women stories were true,<br />but offered no compensation. We didn&#8217;t think<br />it was a proper judgment, while being a barbaric judgment.<br />It not only can&#8217;t convince the victims and their families,<br />but also can&#8217;t convince the suffering people. ”</p>
<p>The Japanese courts pronounced no compensation<br />because statutory limitations had passed.<br />The individual weren&#8217;t allowed prosecuting the government.</p>
<p>Zhang Shuangbing: &#8220;On the statute of limitations-because<br />after the war, China and Japan were in a Cold War&#8212;<br />they did not set up diplomatic relations<br />so there are no conditions to prosecute.<br />Also, as private citizens can&#8217;t prosecute the government, we<br />think, since the Japanese government commanded the invasion of China,<br />why your government can bully people, but the people<br />are not allowed to prosecute Japanese government? &#8220;</p>
<p>Two Japanese non-governmental organizations, Japan Shanxi<br />Identify Committee and Japan Photo Exhibition Committee sent a telegram of condolences to Yin Yulin.<br />They said: &#8220;Aunt&#8217;s wish to get ‘the Japanese government&#8217;s<br />formal apology and compensation, to restore the reputation’<br />before her death failed to achieve this and we feel very guilty."</p>
<p>After Yin Yulin death, in Shanxi, only six comfort women<br />&#8220;living witnesses" remained.</p>
<p>While comfort women are seeking Japanese government<br />compensation, the CCP remained silent.<br />They didn&#8217;t make representations to the Japanese government.</p>
<p>The Chinese folk Diaoyu Islands Federation members Feng<br />Jinghua said he could not understand the practice of the CCP.</p>
<p>Diaoyu personage Feng Jinghua: &#8220;Why does China not do<br />these things? We are also very puzzled, we are very depressed.<br />We also hope that the relevant state departments<br />pay attention to this matter.<br />The victims are involved very widely, once it is mentioned<br />Along with such issues as labor, the chemical weapons issue,<br />the comfort women issue, then some people might think<br />that it will destroy Sino-Japanese relations and Sino-Japanese friendship."</p>
<p>Feng Jinghua said that every year, Diaoyu personage visit<br />the old people, but their number decreased every year.<br />In their view, the CCP and the Japanese government<br />hope these people to die as soon as possible.<br />They regard those people as a burden, especially the CCP.</p>
<p>They are blindly waiting, watching them silently die. They have<br />no living benefits such as pension and medical relief, either.</p>
<p>Feng jinghua: &#8220;When we denounced the Japanese,<br />we should reflect on our Chinese people ourselves.<br />Why have we Chinese victims lived so miserably?<br />Why have Japanese victimizer lived so well?<br />I also have seen the Japanese soldiers, including victims<br />of the Hiroshima bomb; the Japanese government took good care of them.<br />But how are our Chinese victims treated? These old people<br />have no health insurance, no other insurance.<br />This is the result of our current social situation in China."</p>
<p>By contrast, on Oct.1st, for the first time, the South Korean<br />government proposed the issue of comfort women in the keynote speech of the General Assembly of the U.N.<br />On Aug. 30th the Korean Constitutional Tribunal claimed<br />judgment on the issue of former comfort women prosecuting the government for compensation.<br />The judgment pronounced that the Government violated<br />relevant provisions of the Constitution and<br />no serious negotiations with the Japanese government<br />in dealing with the former comfort women.<br />Subsequently, the South Korean government asked<br />the Japanese government to carry out agreement<br />between the two governments on issues of<br />comfort women&#8217;s compensation.</p>
