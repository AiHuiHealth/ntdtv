<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西安网民自称物资充足 网友一查是市政府家属院</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/GettyImages-1237453209-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2021年12月28日，空无一人的西安市区。(STR/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2021年12月30日讯】人口1300万的西安市仓促封城又封门，大量民众开始挨饿，网上民怨沸腾。日前一名西安网民宣称自己小区蔬菜充足又便宜，但网友查出是其所在小区是市政府机关家属院。</p>
<p>根据网传聊天截图，这名西安网民在一篇曝光西安<ahref="https://github.com/zrbvdq3366/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/物资供应不足.md#1">物资供应不足</a>的帖文后留言，宣称自己小区的物业一车又一车地拉来蔬菜，白菜才1.5元（人民币，下同）一斤，并称“我们并没有买不到菜”、“您不要再黑西安了”。</p>
<p>网友让此人公布自己的小区名称，该人很快答复“永松路3号院”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103306207" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHtAsm-WUAI_OHq-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103306207" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHtAsm-WUAI_OHq-1-600x1333.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="887" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
<p>在百度地图上查询可见，西安市永松路3号院是市政府机关小区。很多网友纷纷贴出自己的查询结果，并讥讽“再饿不能饿领导”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103306209" style="width: 404px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHtAsm9XwAYN982.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-103306209" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHtAsm9XwAYN982-600x426.jpg" alt="" width="404" b="287" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">百度搜索结果。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>上述永松路3号院的网民宣称白菜价1.5元一斤。但据官媒报导，早在12月23日封城前，西安的白菜市场价就涨到了3.68元一斤。网友指，白菜1.5元一斤显然是中共政府部门的特供价。</p>
<p>根据当地网友爆料，有的普通市民居住的小区内，官方指定蔬菜供应商，价格高达每箱438元，物业还威胁居民称，从其它渠道购买者一律“黄码处理”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103306210" style="width: 386px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHuQg5AUUAAWajh.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-103306210" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHuQg5AUUAAWajh.jpg" alt="" width="386" b="680" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103306230" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHuEVFYVgAMz1Al-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103306230" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHuEVFYVgAMz1Al-1-600x619.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="464" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
<h2>西安食品供应不足 挨饿民众怨声载道</h2>
<p>西安仓促封城多日，目前已进入“足不出户”的封门阶段。许多民众物资储备不足，官方供应又跟不上，市民纷纷抱怨开始挨饿。</p>
<p>12月27日，当地官媒直播省政府防疫发布会，大量市民涌入留言，要求当局“安排小区卖菜”，官媒很快关闭了评论功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103306215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHuoPVUVgAIQH2d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103306215" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHuoPVUVgAIQH2d-600x619.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="619" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大量市民涌入要求官方保障物资供应，党媒直播随即关闭评论。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大陆社交媒体上，出现大量西安市民的求助帖文。当地网民表示，小区供应的食品不但价格高昂，而且供应严重不足，有钱都买不到，连线上商店都已订不到货，只能在家中挨饿。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHrG1ghUYAErjOe.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103306216 aligncenter" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHrG1ghUYAErjOe.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="867" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHrG1ghVUAU0O2a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103306217 aligncenter" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHrG1ghVUAU0O2a.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="869" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103306218" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHtNVV5VgAAIK_X.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103306218" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHtNVV5VgAAIK_X-600x1200.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="800" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有网友透露，有人已经饿得开始吃猫肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103306219" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHrLzgZakAEbnGh.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103306219" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHrLzgZakAEbnGh-600x408.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="271" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
<p>在残酷的现实面前，许多此前选择“相信政府”的民众开始绝望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103306220" style="width: 494px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHrDbgjVUAMUF1c.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-103306220" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/FHrDbgjVUAMUF1c.jpg" alt="" width="494" b="261" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
