<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：新年来临 盘点中国各式年夜菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/a5e120f3f7bf4de4a54d3def05fbedb8-800x450.jpeg" />
<h6>中国各式年夜菜。（网络截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2021年02月11日讯】中国传统新年来临，由于受疫情影响，各地都要求民众保持社交距离，过年的气氛没有往年那么浓郁，但准备一桌色香味俱全的年夜菜，却是必不可少的。</p>
<p>对中国人来说，年夜饭有着特殊的意义。它不仅象征着团圆和亲情，从古至今更是一种独有的文化传承。以下为大家盘点中国各式年夜菜，来感受一下中国的饮食文化。</p>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/virneq315/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/清蒸鲈鱼.md#1">清蒸鲈鱼</a></strong></p>
<p>鱼，寓意美好，象征年年有余。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051173" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/60fac5bbc23347aa8d5428fe088083f6-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>卤全鸡</strong></p>
<p>俗话说“无鸡不宴”，一桌完整的宴席中总少不了鸡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051177" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051177" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4f615eb1ec3845ebb6b9a697e9d94292-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/virneq315/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/梅菜扣肉.md#1">梅菜扣肉</a></strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/virneq315/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/梅菜扣肉.md#1">梅菜扣肉</a>是一道传统菜，色泽红润油亮，香气四溢，扣肉醇香，肥而不腻，老人小孩都爱吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051167" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/f1e1fbc3f6094a85a9b12d04870889bf-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>酱烧猪肘子</strong></p>
<p>酱烧肘子是年夜饭上少不了的一道大菜，颜色红亮、酥烂醇香，集色香味于一身。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051172" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8926ad1ac95641c7bb41b10e12970c3f-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>黄豆焖猪脚</strong></p>
<p>猪蹄有“挠钱聚财”的美好寓意，所以年夜饭的餐桌必定少不了一道猪蹄大菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051175" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3d7d1675db63493b95e55fa8726a7dc9-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>鲜虾粉丝煲</strong></p>
<p>粉丝吸足大虾的鲜香，大虾肉质紧实弹牙，两者搭配起来，简直绝了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051168" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/ea8249a3a3954d4cabfc7d806021de4e-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>莲藕排骨汤</strong></p>
<p>莲藕排骨汤作为汤中佳品，味道鲜美十足，既能吃肉又能喝汤，可谓是一举两得。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051174" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/0024b1f233544aefb3f9de1af9bac477-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>红烧狮子头</strong></p>
<p>红烧狮子头，也就是肉丸子，有团团圆圆的美好寓意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051178" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/e82cfb79b9e2b6cf-600x445.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="445" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>皮蛋拌豆腐</strong></p>
<p>豆腐和皮蛋是经典的搭配，豆腐软嫩滑口，皮蛋自带特殊“香气”，是年夜饭餐桌难得的清凉爽口之味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051166" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/ffd44275008b4b6387d170e5cfc73064-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>凉拌猪耳朵</strong></p>
<p>年夜饭少不了几道下酒菜，凉拌猪耳朵吃起来清脆可口，味道麻辣鲜香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051171" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/a243da8bc9d448a594cf244dbe2407ad-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>风味茄子</strong></p>
<p>风味茄子色泽油亮，口感外脆里嫩，入口酸甜焦香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051170" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/b84a871d160849fa948d150d4d1908b1-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>双椒杏鲍菇</strong></p>
<p>杏鲍菇味道鲜美，搭配青红椒丝，香辣与鲜美并存，入口滑嫩十足，虽是素菜，滋味丝毫不输荤菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103051179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103051179" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/e0eeb15947d34793aae824bdbf19580f-1-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
