<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超吸睛运动健将 “东奥七美”成娇点</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/Capture-108-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>加拿大七项全能选手艾儿伍德(Georgia Ellenwood)。(图片: @george_ahhh IG)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2021年07月29日讯】2020<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/东京奥运.md#1">东京奥运</a>正式登场，各个比赛项目的成绩也陆续揭晓中，而除了竞争激烈的赛事外，许多选手也成了“娇点”，她们不仅有惊人的运动实力，还拥有超吸睛的美丽容貌。</p>
<p>德国田径选手施密特(Alica Schmidt)是2020<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/东京奥运.md#1">东京奥运</a>最受瞩目的“娇点”，金发碧眼的她身高176公分，曾被《Busted Coverage》杂志评选为“世界上最性感的运动员”， 而外型出众的施密特也受到许多厂商的青睐，但她志在田径运动， 因此拒绝了代言邀约。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CQ33fQ2srs9/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=addbeb3f-17fe-4d50-902b-c17ac869b3f0">（相关图片）</a><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CRepumGsp_X/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=c3b31cc8-e4a2-4f61-9b71-9633e01e9a11">（相关图片）</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103177309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103177309" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/1-395-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">德国田径选手施密特(Alica Schmidt)（右二）资料照（Oliver Hardt/Getty Images for European Athletics)</figcaption></figure>
<p>出身于柔道世家的乌克兰柔道选手比洛季德(Daria Bilodid)，她拥有令人羡慕的九头身及迷人的甜美笑容， 在2018年世界柔道锦标赛中曾夺下女子48公斤级冠军，成为史上最年轻的冠军得主。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CRGVV99D3m1/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=91e41d6e-813b-438e-b471-24d338fe9775">（相关图片）</a><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CRwGGhYj26Q/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=a6c1326f-746e-4248-955c-0189dceabfc9">（相关图片）</a><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CQq4qbKjA-L/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=cb0a6323-68ed-4e5d-a9e4-8f139c122a96">（相关图片）</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103177312" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103177312" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/1-396-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">乌克兰柔道选手比洛季德(Daria Bilodid) 资料照（Kiyoshi Ota/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在球场上有着“小钢炮”外号的意大利网球选手吉奥尔吉(Camila Giorgi)，是个性感的金发甜姐儿， 外形宛如真人芭比的她是时尚品牌的最爱。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CR2XiTysGI5/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=d7bb4e56-139e-4907-87d1-b48b35eb5db9">（相关图片）</a><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CRI5-YsM6jP/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=bbbba3a4-a068-4784-a97b-f3c17031fd8b">（相关图片）</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103177283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103177283" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/1-391-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">意大利网球选手吉奥尔吉(Camila Giorgi)外形宛如真人芭比（Clive Brunskill/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>七项全能选手艾儿伍德(Georgia Ellenwood)这次被加拿大队视为夺牌热门，身高170公分的她笑起来有着一双迷濛的双眼，私下穿搭风格多变气势完全不输超模。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CQ3imQ4hkph/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=78e953ba-3dd1-44fc-b9ad-cd710a79230e">（相关图片）</a><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CP6UwwahGOl/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=f8f6830f-3b05-43ab-a9bf-d51d344fe1a5">（相关图片）</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103177320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103177320" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/1-397-600x485.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="485" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">七项全能选手加拿大艾儿伍德(Georgia Ellenwood)在（右一）资料照（DON EMMERT/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>斯洛维尼亚攀岩选手甘布蕾特(Janja Garnbret)8岁时就开始接受正规的攀登运动训练， 后来更是世界攀岩锦标赛的常胜军， 甘布蕾特这次是争夺奥运首面攀岩金牌的热门人物，拥有美貌的她将再次展现惊人的运动实力。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CRHZPMFjg6k/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=1be19c96-0d7a-4b29-b04f-6ded3b14578f">（相关图片）</a><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CQwPLrrDUD0/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=f5456a0c-b7f8-45f4-91d1-af10c8853498">（相关图片）</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103177322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103177322" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/1-398-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">斯洛维尼亚攀岩选手甘布蕾特(Janja Garnbret)资料照（Sebastian Widmann/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>23岁的乌克兰跳高女神列夫琴科(Yuliya Levchenko)身高179公分，高颜值的她有着一双大长腿，在多次的竞赛中表现相当亮眼，被誉为天才型的选手。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CRMY4qfr8nc/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=0164f87d-87c1-4a1c-b830-8a4b3ae0ea19">（相关图片）</a><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CPf3MlgH-tc/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=654eb2e2-271f-4b11-923d-99a81b36afa2">（相关图片）</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103177324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103177324" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/1-399-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">乌克兰跳高女神列夫琴科(Yuliya Levchenko)资料照（Oliver Hardt/Getty Images for European Athletics)</figcaption></figure>
<p>日本乒乓球选手石川佳纯出身体育世家,她在日本拥有高人气,长相甜美的石川佳纯是日本保养品牌的代言大使。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/COu9sStjL1O/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=a02ab019-a41d-4953-8ceb-32b1a3e55112">（相关图片）</a><ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CQdH0fdDFwk/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;ig_rid=11cc1607-0a82-41b2-87c9-cc5727aeb60d">（相关图片）</a></p>
