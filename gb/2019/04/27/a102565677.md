	<p>Job Deaion: Responsible for monitoring, analyzing, and evaluating operation performance and results. Provide analytical support in the creation of media campaigns to deliver efficiency improvements and increasing quality initiatives. Synthesize and model analysis results to an executive audience of management teams. Analyze financial reports and data related to operations, productivity, and quality to identify opportunities for program management teams.</p>
<p>Prioritize business intelligence needs for partners. Data quality validation. Work in support of program teams in the U.S. and globally in providing strategic data modeling for their undertaking of media campaigns.</p>
<p>Job Requirements:</p>
<p>Bachelor’s (or foreign equivalent) degree in Economics, Finance or related;one year of experience in the job offered or as an Asset Servicing Analyst or related;</p>
<p>Some work experience required with: Stata, Google Analytics, and Tableau; Financial/quantitative analysis tools such as R/Splus, Stata, and Python; and SQL coding &amp; databases.</p>
