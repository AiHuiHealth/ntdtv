<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第91届奥斯卡颁奖典礼 群星云集闪耀红毯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/GettyImages-1127144102-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>第91届奥斯卡金像奖红毯，华裔影星杨紫琼。（Neilson Barnard/Getty Images）</h6>
