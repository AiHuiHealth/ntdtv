<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>丁海寅亲切台语问好 金钟新生代男星展帅气</h2>
<img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/1-100-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>韩星丁海寅于10月5日在台北出席“第54届电视金钟奖”彩排记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2019年10月06日讯】韩星<a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/丁海寅.md">丁海寅</a>5日登台出席第54届金钟奖，身穿时尚全黑西装搭配俐落油头亮相，以台语亲切打招呼，全场尖叫声爆满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102680257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102680257" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/2-25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/丁海寅.md">丁海寅</a>身穿全黑西装，搭配俐落油头亮相，全场尖叫声爆满，以台语亲切打招呼。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_102680258" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-102680258" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/3-16.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">丁海寅身穿全黑西装，搭配俐落油头亮相，全场尖叫声爆满，以台语亲切打招呼。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/张轩睿.md">张轩睿</a>穿着深绿丝绒西装，古典绅风；<a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/周兴哲.md">周兴哲</a>将救生衣西装穿出型男时尚，期待未来能从歌手升格MV导演；<a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/刘以豪.md">刘以豪</a>身穿墨绿西装，衬托腼腆笑容，访问时还有热情粉丝丢掷礼物进场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102680259" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-102680259" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/4-10.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/张轩睿.md">张轩睿</a>穿着深绿丝绒西装，古典绅风。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_102680260" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-102680260" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/5-7.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/周兴哲.md">周兴哲</a>将救生衣西装穿出型男时尚，期待未来能从歌手升格MV导演。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_102680261" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-102680261" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/6-9.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/刘以豪.md">刘以豪</a>身穿墨绿西装，衬托腼腆笑容。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>获奖呼声高的<a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/温昇豪.md">温昇豪</a>，身穿经典端正的正式西装；金钟视帝吴慷仁一出场让众人惊呼认不出来，他为戏改变为黝黑肤色与光头造型，展现演员的敬业态度与可塑性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102680262" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-102680262" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/7-5.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/clbjqp2826/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/温昇豪.md">温昇豪</a>身穿正式西装，经典端正。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_102680263" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-102680263" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/8-4.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吴慷仁为戏改变为黝黑肤色与光头造型，特别造型让众人惊呼，也展现演员的敬业态度与可塑性。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林柏昇（KID）特别向军中告假力挺出席金钟典礼，与黄鸿升（小鬼）搭档走星光大道，他兴奋喊话：“再半年就要出来和大家见面了，现在正在努力减肥。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_102680264" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-102680264" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/9-5.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">林柏昇（KID）特别向军中告假力挺出席金钟典礼。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
