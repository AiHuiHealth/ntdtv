<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>落入凡尘 带给凡间一抹宁静的美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-13-600x398-1-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>石头图腾（Instagram@sculpttheworld）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2020年02月14日讯】人为凡事忙，现代社会的快节奏让人很少有闲暇能够得到一丝真正的平和与安静，而忙于俗世的人们，似乎在这样的快节奏中也忘记了寻求平和的本能。</p>
<p>Jon Foreman生活在英国威尔士彭布罗克郡，还在读大学的时候，他在繁忙的学业之余突然发觉到了来自大自然美的馈赠——<ahref="https://github.com/lsouru216/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/石头.md#1">石头</a>。</p>
<p>一颗颗散落的<ahref="https://github.com/lsouru216/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/鹅卵石.md#1">鹅卵石</a>，将它们错落有致的结合在一起，也能成为一次次视觉与灵魂的碰撞。</p>
<p>Jon生活的彭布罗克郡附近有广阔的海岸线，所以这里的<ahref="https://github.com/lsouru216/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/鹅卵石.md#1">鹅卵石</a>成了最原始、最廉价的创作工具，各种大小、形状、颜色的<ahref="https://github.com/lsouru216/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/石头.md#1">石头</a>应有尽有。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102777712" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102777712" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-202-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石头<ahref="https://github.com/lsouru216/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/图腾.md#1">图腾</a>（Instagram@sculpttheworld）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将这些石头摆放在一起的过程中，Jon能够忘记来自生活、学习的所有压力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102777733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102777733" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-46-600x489.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="489" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石头<ahref="https://github.com/lsouru216/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/图腾.md#1">图腾</a>（Instagram@sculpttheworld）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">而他的作品，也带着他灵魂中的那抹淡然，让观赏者的心灵也得到片刻的宁静。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_102777715" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102777715" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-24-600x603.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="603" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石头图腾（Instagram@sculpttheworld）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对称和旋转之美是Jon作品的核心图腾，如这种从中心向四周扩散的美仿佛是我们这个银河系在地球上的缩影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102777716" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102777716" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-14-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石头图腾（Instagram@sculpttheworld）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_102777717" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102777717" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-13-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石头图腾（Instagram@sculpttheworld）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_102777718" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102777718" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-12-600x601.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="601" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石头图腾（Instagram@sculpttheworld）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着创作机会的增多和灵感的爆发，Jon不再满足于石头的平面艺术，他利用沙滩的可塑性，将石头与3D结合在一起，一幅幅立体作品就呈现在我们眼前。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102777719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102777719" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-7-600x498.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="498" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石头图腾（Instagram@sculpttheworld）</figcaption></figure>
