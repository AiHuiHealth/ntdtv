<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>用红酒酱烤的彩色胡萝卜（组图）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2020-11-12_143944-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>用红酒酱和百里香酱烤的胡萝卜(Victoria de la Maza)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>红葡萄酒和百里香酱烤的<ahref="https://github.com/myjazr3374/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/彩色胡萝卜.md#1">彩色胡萝卜</a>，在简单烤制的<ahref="https://github.com/myjazr3374/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/胡萝卜.md#1">胡萝卜</a>上，撒上一种甜的、百里香汁和<ahref="https://github.com/myjazr3374/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红酒酱.md#1">红酒酱</a>，在炉灶上煮至糖浆状。</p>
<p>我挑选了不同颜色的<ahref="https://github.com/myjazr3374/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/胡萝卜.md#1">胡萝卜</a>，它们看起来有节日气氛而且味道鲜美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102985150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2020-11-12_151111-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" class="size-medium wp-image-102985150" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">各种颜色的胡<ahref="https://github.com/myjazr3374/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/萝卜.md#1">萝卜</a>（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>服务6至8人份：</strong></p>
<p>2磅细的<ahref="https://github.com/myjazr3374/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/彩色胡萝卜.md#1">彩色胡萝卜</a>，去皮</p>
<p>2汤匙橄榄油</p>
<p>2瓣大蒜，切碎</p>
<p>1/2茶匙海盐</p>
<p>1/2茶匙现磨黑胡椒</p>
<p><strong>酱料：</strong></p>
<p>1 1/2杯干红葡萄酒，例如梅洛葡萄酒</p>
<p>4小枝新鲜百里香，再加上些许小枝作装饰</p>
<p>1瓣大蒜，切碎</p>
<figure id="attachment_102985153" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2020-11-12_151143-600x345.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="345" class="size-medium wp-image-102985153" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">彩色胡<ahref="https://github.com/myjazr3374/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/萝卜.md#1">萝卜</a>做成美食（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>作法：</strong></p>
<p>将烤箱预热至400华氏度。</p>
<p>在一个大碗中，将胡萝卜，橄榄油，大蒜，盐和胡椒粉混合在一起。</p>
<p>将胡萝卜单层铺在烤盘上。烘烤约30分钟，直到外部焦糖，但内部仍然有点脆。</p>
<p>同时用一小锅小火将红葡萄酒、百里香和大蒜一起搅拌混合。慢火煮至减少1/4，具有淡糖浆或液体焦糖的稠度。</p>
<p>从<ahref="https://github.com/myjazr3374/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红酒酱.md#1">红酒酱</a>里取出百里香小枝，倒在胡萝卜上。</p>
<p>用鲜百里香点缀，趁热吃。</p>
<p>（责任编辑：张莉）</p>
