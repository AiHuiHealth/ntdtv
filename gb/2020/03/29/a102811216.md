<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>千瑞：天的谴告? 人的机会</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p class="p2"><span class="s1"><b>天的<ahref="https://github.com/roxfi2557/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/谴告.md#1">谴告</a>? 人的机会</b></span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">?</span><span class="s1">作者：千瑞</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">?</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">古人有训：</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">国家之失，出灾异以<ahref="https://github.com/roxfi2557/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/谴告.md#1">谴告</a>之</span><span class="s2">。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">世人，哪有无因无由的休戚？</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">古人有教：</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">天祚圣明，见怪异以惊骇之。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">世上，哪有无缘无故的霹雳？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">看历史——</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">耶稣，来到红尘中传道救人。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">神啊，被人钉在了十字架上。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">这是怎样的恶？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">这有多大的罪？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">为此，迫害神的国，</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">历尽了三次的瘟疫，家国灭落。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">人啊，人！</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">这历史的教训，你是否记得？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">为此，迫害神的人，</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">偿尽了千年的苦难，风雨漂泊。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">人啊，人！</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">这其中的启示，你是否懂得？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">望中原——</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">创世的主，转法轮，救世人。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">祸世的魔，施迫害，毁世人。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">二十年腥风血雨：</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">大法被污蔑，污泥浊水倾天恶洒。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">你可知道，</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">多少大法弟子被酷刑和虐杀？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">二十年大罪奇恶：</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">大法被迫害，魔叫鬼舞遍地血沥。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">你可知道，</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">多少大法弟子被失踪和活摘？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">如果有人出来揭发，江泽民曾利用军警，</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">把五百名大法修炼者集体投入钢水沸腾的炼钢炉……</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">这样的邪恶，你敢相信？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">这样的罪孽，你不惊震？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1"><ahref="https://github.com/roxfi2557/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a>骂神，还要欺骗人去骂神。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">江鬼弑神，还要蛊惑人去弑神。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">今日之中原，罪恶之孽海。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">乌鸦飞正欢，阴霾落不怠。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">人在黑暗中，人在迷乱中。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">只要还有一线希望，大法就要救度你。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">人啊，人！</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">这当世的善恶，你是否选得？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">人在受虐中，人在被害中。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">只要还有一刻时光，大法绝不丢下你。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">人啊，人！</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">这面对的是非，你是否辨得？</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">快醒来：</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">仰望天的至尊，读懂天的洪恩。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">快起来：</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">人本来自于天，人应回归于天。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">快啊，退出<ahref="https://github.com/roxfi2557/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a>——</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">世人正面对千年重生的大门！</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">看啊，天灭中共——</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">中共正灭作一地可耻的灰尘！</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">?</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">注：⑴推荐阅读《共产主义的终极目的》和《魔鬼在统治着我们的世界》两本书。⑵三退，即声明退出中共党、团、队，详情见大纪元首页“三退”专栏。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">?</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">2020年3月28日</span></p>
