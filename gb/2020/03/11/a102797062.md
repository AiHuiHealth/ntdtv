<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>武汉肺炎 巴拿马报死亡病例 牙买加、玻利维亚失守</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/GettyImages-1206473687-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>Handout picture released by Panama&#039;s Health Ministry presss office showing members of the Health Ministry waiting for tourists on arrival at Port of Amador in Panama City, on March 10, 2020. - The Panamanian government announced the first case of COVID-19 in the country, in a woman who arrived from Spain. (Photo by Panama&#039;s Health Ministry presss Office / AFP) (Photo by PANAMA&#039;S HEALTH MINISTRY PRESSS /AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2020年03月11日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/巴拿马.md#1">巴拿马</a>卫生部10日(周二)证实境内出现8例<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/武汉肺炎.md#1">武汉肺炎</a>确诊病例，其中1人不治身亡，是中美洲地区首度通报相关死亡病例。另外，<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/牙买加.md#1">牙买加</a>及<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/玻利维亚.md#1">玻利维亚</a>出现首次病例。</p>
<p>卫生部长特纳（Rosario Turner）记者会上表示，通报8人确诊<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/武汉肺炎.md#1">武汉肺炎</a>，一人正在加护病房，另一人不幸死亡。</p>
<p>死者为64岁男性，生前罹患与细菌性肺炎有关的糖尿病并发症毒。其余7例确诊者年龄在29至59岁间，近日都有出国史。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/巴拿马.md#1">巴拿马</a>是继哥斯大黎加后，第2个通报武汉肺炎病毒确诊病例的中美洲国家。巴拿马政府下令，国内受影响地区的公私立学校都将停课到4月7日。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102797088" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/GettyImages-1206473685-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" class="size-medium wp-image-102797088" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020年3月10日，卫生部成员在巴拿马阿马多尔港为乘客量体温。(PANAMA&#8217;S HEALTH MINISTRY PRESSS /AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>哥斯大黎加13人采检呈阳性反应，包括3名美国人。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/牙买加.md#1">牙买加</a>首宗个案中，患者是一名自英国返国的女子。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/玻利维亚.md#1">玻利维亚</a>新增的病例中，患者为两名分别60岁及64岁的女子。近日都曾有到访欧洲重灾区意大利，之后分别在玻利维亚奥鲁罗及圣克鲁斯省确诊。</p>
<p>(责任编辑：卢勇信)</p>
