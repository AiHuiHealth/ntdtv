<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>快速自制樱桃冰淇淋（组图）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2020-08-29_150445-800x450.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>这种快速<ahref="https://github.com/twfknp314/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/自制.md#1">自制</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/twfknp314/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/樱桃.md#1">樱桃</a><ahref="https://github.com/twfknp314/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/冰淇淋.md#1">冰淇淋</a>，可贮存很久，想要吃的时候随时拿出来吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102929127" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2020-08-29_145906-600x341.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="341" class="size-medium wp-image-102929127" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">快速简单的<ahref="https://github.com/twfknp314/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/自制.md#1">自制</a><ahref="https://github.com/twfknp314/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/冰淇淋.md#1">冰淇淋</a>，示意图（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>制作时可根据你所需要的甜度来选择炼乳与淡奶的比例，两罐淡奶就可制成非常甜的冰淇淋，而三罐淡奶就更甜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102929128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2020-08-29_145931-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" class="size-medium wp-image-102929128" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">奶的比例可根据甜度来选择（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果您没有时间使用冰淇淋机来制作，可将混合物冷冻在冰块托盘或迷你松饼锅中，然后将冷冻的混合物放在快速食品处理机中，以进行快速处理。 </p>
<figure id="attachment_102929129" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2020-08-29_150024-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-medium wp-image-102929129" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">漂亮的<ahref="https://github.com/twfknp314/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/樱桃.md#1">樱桃</a>很美味（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>制作6人份所需的材料：</strong></p>
<p>炼乳 2罐</p>
<p>淡奶 2-3罐</p>
<p>去核和梗的马拉斯奇诺樱桃 1小罐</p>
<figure id="attachment_102929131" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2020-08-29_150139-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" class="size-medium wp-image-102929131" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美味樱桃示意图（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>作法：</strong></p>
<p>1、在搅拌机中混合炼乳和淡奶。</p>
<p>2、将樱桃罐中的液体加到混合物中充分搅拌混合。</p>
<p>3、将樱桃切成所需的大小，然后添加到牛奶中，或者将它们放入搅拌机中，然后搅拌几次。</p>
<p>4、冷却混合物直至成形。 </p>
<figure id="attachment_102929132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2020-08-29_150341-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" class="size-medium wp-image-102929132" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">两颗去核和梗的马拉斯奇诺樱桃（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_102929134" style="width: 481px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2020-08-29_150412.jpg" alt="" width="481" b="625" class="size-full wp-image-102929134" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">这颗樱桃没有去核和梗，一样美味。（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_102929139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2020-08-29_150237-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" class="size-medium wp-image-102929139" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">这颗樱桃没有去核和梗，一样美味。（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
