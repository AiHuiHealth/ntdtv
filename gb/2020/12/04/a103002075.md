<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>番茄乾和石榴沙拉 增添假日欢乐的光彩（组图）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2020-12-04_155310-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>番茄乾和石榴沙拉 (David Hagerman)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2020年12月04日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/zwhoec341/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/番茄干.md#1">番茄干</a>、橄榄油和石榴，做成<ahref="https://github.com/zwhoec341/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/沙拉.md#1">沙拉</a>，通常将其作为开胃菜或用作早餐。</p>
<p><strong>提供6至8人份材料：</strong></p>
<p>晒干的西红柿 20个</p>
<p>中等大小的石榴 1个，水平切成两半，取籽</p>
<p>辣椒 1个，例如墨西哥胡椒，切成薄片</p>
<p>新鲜薄荷或平叶欧芹 1/4杯，切碎</p>
<p>淡咸白色奶酪 1/4杯，碎的</p>
<p>果味橄榄油 1/4杯</p>
<p>石榴糖蜜 1汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_103002099" style="width: 549px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-full wp-image-103002099" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2020-12-04_155232.jpg" alt="" width="549" b="365" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石榴取籽做成<ahref="https://github.com/zwhoec341/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/沙拉.md#1">沙拉</a>(New Africa/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<p>晒干的西红柿在温水中浸泡10至20分钟，直到变软。</p>
<p>当西红柿浸泡时，将石榴去籽。</p>
<p>沥干晒干的西红柿，切成薄片，切成1/4英寸宽的条。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103002103" style="width: 428px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="size-full wp-image-103002103" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2020-12-04_155416.jpg" alt="" width="428" b="594" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">晒干的西红柿（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将番茄条放在一个小盘子上，撒上辣椒片。</p>
<p>撒上薄荷，再撒上奶酪和石榴籽。</p>
<p>淋上橄榄油和石榴糖蜜，立即食用。</p>
<p>（责任编辑：张莉）</p>
