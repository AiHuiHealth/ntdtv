<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>清爽甘甜“豆腐汤”早上迅速煮好高汤的秘诀！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-17_154042-800x450.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>早上让味觉苏醒的“清甜营养<ahref="https://github.com/lhvhzm364/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐汤.md#1">豆腐汤</a>”跟一般的<ahref="https://github.com/lhvhzm364/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/泡菜.md#1">泡菜</a>豆腐锅比起来，这道豆腐汤口味清爽，对健康不会造成负担，也不必另外准备一碗白饭，就能吃饱又吃得巧。早上迅速煮好<ahref="https://github.com/lhvhzm364/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/高汤.md#1">高汤</a>的秘诀是前一晚事先煮好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102965254" style="width: 465px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-17_154025.jpg" alt="" width="465" b="352" class="size-full wp-image-102965254" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/lhvhzm364/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐汤.md#1">豆腐汤</a>起锅后放凉，搭配调味料就可以上桌。（图／采实文化提供）<br /></figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>必要材料（2人份）</strong></p>
<p>蒜苗5cm<br />
小鱼干5尾<br />
豆腐1块</p>
<p><strong>调味料</strong></p>
<p>辣椒粉 1汤匙<br />
酱油 2汤匙<br />
葱 1汤匙<br />
蒜泥 1汤匙<br />
芝麻油少许</p>
<p><strong>步骤</strong></p>
<p>1.先调好调味料，将蒜苗斜切。（在前一晚就准备好）</p>
<figure id="attachment_102965253" style="width: 459px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-17_154015.jpg" alt="" width="459" b="348" class="size-full wp-image-102965253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前一晚就可以先调好酱汁；辣椒粉、酱油、葱、蒜泥、芝麻油。（图／采实文化提供）<br /></figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>豆腐汤</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_102965252" style="width: 462px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-17_154001.jpg" alt="" width="462" b="354" class="size-full wp-image-102965252" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前一晚先备妥蒜苗5公分长然后斜切。（图／采实文化提供）<br /></figcaption></figure>
<p>2.锅内加2杯水和5尾小鱼干煮5分钟，水滚之后把小鱼干捞起就可以跑去睡觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102965251" style="width: 462px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-17_153951.jpg" alt="" width="462" b="353" class="size-full wp-image-102965251" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">早上不用花时间煮<ahref="https://github.com/lhvhzm364/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/高汤.md#1">高汤</a>的秘诀就是“前一晚先煮好”！只要在锅子加2杯水、5尾小鱼干煮5分钟，水滚之后把小鱼干捞起就可以跑去睡觉。（图／采实文化提供）<br /></figcaption></figure>
<p>3.把豆腐、蒜苗放进高汤里，用大火滚煮2分钟。</p>
<p><strong>豆腐</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_102965250" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-17_153938.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="353" class="size-full wp-image-102965250" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">早上起来，将高汤取出放入豆腐、蒜苗，用大火滚煮2分钟。（图／采实文化提供）<br /></figcaption></figure>
<p>4.起锅后稍微放凉，跟搭配的调味料一起上桌。（加入一点捏碎的海苔也不错！）</p>
<p><本文摘自：10分钟做早餐：一个人吃、两人吃、全家吃都充满幸福的120道早餐提案，采实文化提供></p>
<p>（转自大纪元/责任编辑：张信燕）</p>
