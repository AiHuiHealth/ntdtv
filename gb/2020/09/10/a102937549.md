<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自制美味红辣酱（组图）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2020-09-10_163350-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>美味红辣酱（Anna Ewa Bieniek / Shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这种<ahref="https://github.com/dbwccs309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/自制.md#1">自制</a>的辣<ahref="https://github.com/dbwccs309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/酱.md#1">酱</a>与杂货店货架上的许多辣酱有什么大的不同？这种酱汁未经发酵，因此保质期短得多。需在一周之内食用或冷冻这种调味料。</p>
<p>如果要制作绿色辣<ahref="https://github.com/dbwccs309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/酱.md#1">酱</a>，请用12个墨西哥绿胡椒代替卡宴红<ahref="https://github.com/dbwccs309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣椒.md#1">辣椒</a>。制作时加入哈瓦那辣椒或泰国胡椒粉以够辣。哦，还要注意在接触辣椒时戴手套保护您的手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102937561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2020-09-10_163824-600x378.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="378" class="size-medium wp-image-102937561" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">漂亮的新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/dbwccs309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣椒.md#1">辣椒</a>（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>制作2杯红<ahref="https://github.com/dbwccs309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣椒酱.md#1">辣椒酱</a>所需的材料：</strong></p>
<p>卡宴辣椒 15个</p>
<p>苹果醋 1杯</p>
<p>大蒜 2瓣，去皮</p>
<p>糖 1/2茶匙</p>
<p>盐 1/4茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_102937559" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2020-09-10_163511-600x395.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="395" class="size-medium wp-image-102937559" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/dbwccs309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣椒酱.md#1">辣椒酱</a>下饭好美味（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>制作方法：</strong></p>
<p>从辣椒上取下茎，切成两半。</p>
<p>然后将所有食材放入锅中。</p>
<p>将混合物煮沸后减小火煮10至12分钟，直到胡椒和大蒜变软为止。</p>
<p>静置5分钟，然后将混合物倒入搅拌机中，搅拌器上面盖一块布，搅拌混合物直到光滑。 </p>
<p>将做好的辣椒酱混合物倒入罐中冷藏，或分装到可冷冻保存的容器中冷冻，以备后用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102937560" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2020-09-10_161614-600x385.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="385" class="size-medium wp-image-102937560" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">制作好的辣椒酱冷藏起来备用（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
