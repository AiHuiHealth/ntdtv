<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>辣椒醋（组图）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2020-09-11_161744-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>辣椒醋（NDanko / Shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>在消毒过的罐子中装满<ahref="https://github.com/eqtrvx309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣椒.md#1">辣椒</a>，然后加入沸腾的<ahref="https://github.com/eqtrvx309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/醋.md#1">醋</a>，最后将罐头密封。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102938351" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2020-09-11_163853-600x384.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="384" class="size-medium wp-image-102938351" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新鲜小<ahref="https://github.com/eqtrvx309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣椒.md#1">辣椒</a>（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>制作1杯<ahref="https://github.com/eqtrvx309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣椒醋.md#1">辣椒醋</a>所需的材料：</strong></p>
<p>新鲜的小<ahref="https://github.com/eqtrvx309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣.md#1">辣</a>椒 1杯</p>
<p>白<ahref="https://github.com/eqtrvx309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/醋.md#1">醋</a> 1杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_102938357" style="width: 462px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2020-09-11_164327.jpg" alt="" width="462" b="610" class="size-full wp-image-102938357" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">可封密的瓶子很适合做<ahref="https://github.com/eqtrvx309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣椒醋.md#1">辣椒醋</a>（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>作法：</strong></p>
<p>切下<ahref="https://github.com/eqtrvx309/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/辣.md#1">辣</a>椒的茎，然后将它们塞入瓶子中（最好是带有软木塞的瓶子可封密）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102938363" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2020-09-11_161803-600x386.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="386" class="size-medium wp-image-102938363" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">作法很简单，加醋覆盖辣椒，密封就可以了。（Tairalist / Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将醋倒在胡椒上。封密并冷藏静置一周。 </p>
<p>当醋变少时，请添加更多醋以覆盖辣椒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102938360" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2020-09-11_163757-600x297.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="297" class="size-medium wp-image-102938360" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">辣椒示意图（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>（责任编辑：张莉）</p>
