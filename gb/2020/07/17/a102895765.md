<h6>2020年7月10日追查国际更新出版《铁正如山》（Ironclad and Irrefutable Evidence） 。 （追查国际）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2020年07月17日讯】</p>
<p><strong>《铁证如山 》（中、英）</strong><br />
<strong>Ironclad and Irrefutable Evidence </strong></p>
<p>2020年7月10日追查国际更新出版《铁正如山》（Ironclad and Irrefutable Evidence） 。该书分第一、二部，有中、英文二种版本。</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-102895770" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/120281_1594830554-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-102895771" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/120281_1594830554_0-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-102895772" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/120281_1594830554_1-600x395.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="395" /></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-102895773" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/120281_1594830554_2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></p>
<p><strong>简介：</strong></p>
<p>这本书汇集了追查国际十多年来对中共大量活摘<ahref="https://github.com/vzyswn306/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/法轮功学员.md#1">法轮功学员</a>器官群体灭绝罪的主要证据，并详实地介绍了调查背景和资料来源，合理论证了证据之间的逻辑关系。书中包含290个录音证据，764个资料证据。书中配有录音音频MP3和录音文本PDF的二维码下载扫描图标，可直接听、看调查现场录音、录像和浏览记录文本。还有追查国际调查现场录像视频的二维码扫描图标，手机扫描就看观看调查现场录像，可在最短的时间内深度地直观了解这起历史大案的真相。</p>
<p><strong>Introduction:</strong></p>
<p>The “Ironclad and Irrefutable Evidence” brings together the WOIPFG’s key evidence collected over a decade of investigation into the CCP’s crime of organ harvesting from living Falun Gong practitioners in China. It details the investigation background and information sources, and the logical relationship among different pieces of evidence. The book contains 290 recorded testimonies and 2000 more pieces of data evidence. It is equipped with QR code downloading scan icons to access MP3 audio recordings, PDF recorded texts, and WOIPFG onsite investigation video recordings, so readers can scan and directly listen to the investigation recordings, browse the recorded texts, and watch the videos. This helps people to efficiently and deeply understand the truth of this historical case.</p>
<p>本书分二部，第一部，是2006年至2015年的部分调查结果。内容包括对五名中共政治局常委和多名军委副主席、国防部长、军方总后勤部卫生部部长、众多中国器官移植医生、及政法委、“610”、中共法院官员等多方面涉嫌责任人的调查取证的记录等多方面的证据。第二部，是2015年至2020年的部分调查结果。包括2019年12月至2020年1月对中共空军军医大学附属西京医院活摘<ahref="https://github.com/vzyswn306/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/法轮功学员.md#1">法轮功学员</a>器官的调查证据。是全面了解和深入研究中共活摘罪行性质、规模和现状的专著。</p>
<p>The book is divided into two parts. The first part focuses on results of the investigation from 2006 to 2015. Its contents include phone investigative recordings and data evidence involving five incumbent and former standing committee members of the Chinese Communist Party Central Politburo, a vice chairman of the Central Military Commission, a defense minister, a former health minister of the General Logistics Department of the People’s Liberation Army, as well as many transplant surgeons, officials of the Political and Legal Affairs Commission, “610 Office” officials, court officials and so on. The second part is the investigation results from 2015 to 2020. This includes evidence from December 2019 to Jan, 2020 on the investigation of Xijing Hospital Affiliated to the Air Force Medical University. “Ironclad and Irrefutable Evidence” is a monograph that comprehensively understands and thoroughly studies the nature, scale and status of the CCP&#8217;s crime of live organ harvesting.</p>
<p>调查表明：中共活摘法轮功学员器官的罪行从没停止，还在继续，中国仍然存在活人器官供体库。而且 “活摘” 罪恶正向全社会蔓延。调查还发现，中共主动张扬的器官黑市，是中共向来掩盖活摘器官罪恶，运做活人器官库的另一种方式。</p>
<p>WOIPFG’s investigation shows that the Chinese Communist Party’s (CCP) crime of organ harvesting from living Falun Gong practitioners has never stopped, and it continues to this day. Also, living organ donor banks still exist in China. Moreover, the CCP’s publicizing of the so-called organ black market in China is another way for it to cover up its evil crime of live organ harvesting and its operation of living organ banks.</p>
