<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】沈阳二监迫害死至少29名法轮功学员</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2015年2月11日讯】<b>沈阳二监<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/迫害.md#1">迫害</a>死至少29名<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/法轮功学员.md#1">法轮功学员</a> </b></p>
<p>追查<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/迫害.md#1">迫害</a>法轮功国际组织《追查国际》，2月9号发表声明指出，沈阳市第二监狱是中共迫害<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/法轮功学员.md#1">法轮功学员</a>的重要场所。</p>
<p>声明中说，这个监狱迫害法轮功学员的手段异常残忍恶毒，如对法轮功学员用电烤、电棍电、戴手铐坐老虎凳、用铁线拧脚、用打火机烧、高温下曝晒，不准大、小便，不准喝水，不准洗澡，还有超负荷的劳动等等。</p>
<p>经“追查国际”核实确认，被沈阳市第二监狱迫害致死的，有名有姓的法轮功学员就达29人。而由于中共当局的信息封锁，可能还有更多的迫害致死案例，没有得到曝光，实际被迫害致死人数可能会更多。</p>
<p><b> 南开大学校长批教育部长言论被删</b></p>
<p>2月9号，中共党媒“人民网”罕见发表大陆南开大学校长龚克的讲话，明确批评中共教育部长要求的整顿教师队伍是1957年或1966年的思维。</p>
<p>龚克是在参加“人民网”举办的一个在线交流活动时发表的这番讲话，他表示，加强意识形态工作不能走极端，不能重蹈历史上对知识份子“左”的错误。</p>
<p>不过，龚克的这番讲话已经从“人民网”首页消失，只在教育频道上出现，而一些转载报导的大陆媒体，则已经删除了相关内容。</p>
<p>有评论认为，事件显示，中共高层的各派角力已经公开化。</p>
<p><b>港学生领袖将赴联合国人权峰会演讲 </b></p>
<p>香港“占领运动”中的学生领袖周永康和岑敖晖，2月下旬将赴日内瓦参加联合国人权峰会，并发表演讲，大会官方网站已经刊出他们的头像做为宣传，并发出新闻稿，说两人将同伊朗、俄罗斯、朝鲜、古巴、委内瑞拉的人权及民主活跃人士同台演讲。</p>
<p>编辑/周玉林</p>
<p>Confirmed: 29 Falun Gong Practitioners Tortured to Death in Shenyang No.2 Prison</p>
<p>On Feb.9, World Organization to Investigate the Persecution <br />of Falun Gong (WOIPFG), announced the shocking revelations:<br />about how Shenyang No.2 Prison commits heinous crimes <br />of persecuting Falun Gong practitioners.</p>
<p>The announcement revealed extremely brutal and vicious <br />ways of torturing Falun Gong practitioners in the hell hole. <br />Some examples of such torture are, electric roasting, electric <br />shocking, torture rack, iron wire tweaking, forced exposure to sun, <br />prohibition of urination or defecation, prohibition of drinking, <br />prohibition of bathing, forced labor overload , and others.</p>
<p>WOIPFG has named 29 Falun Gong practitioners confirmed <br />to have been tortured to death in Shenyang No.2 Prison. <br />It is likely that there are more deaths covered up by the Chinese <br />Communist Party’s (CCP) information blockade. <br />The true number of victims could be higher than reported, <br />said WOIPFG.</p>
<p>University President’s Open Criticism against Education Minister <br />Removed from Mainland Media</p>
<p>On Monday, Party mouthpiece People’s Daily unusually <br />reported a speech by Gong Ke, the president of Nankai <br />University, on its website. </p>
<p>Gong directly criticized the purification movement requested <br />by the Education Minister as being the mentality of 1957 or 1966. </p>
<p>Gong Ke made the speech in an online discussion held by <br />People’s Daily website. <br />“In enhancing ideological work, we cannot go to another <br />extreme; we cannot re-enact this history of ‘leftist’ errors <br />against intellectuals,” said Gong.</p>
<p>However, Gong’s speech has been removed from the Home <br />Page of People’s Daily, and is only shown on the website’s <br />education channel. </p>
<p>The report was also removed by other mainland media <br />that published the report a short time ago.</p>
<p>Commentators say the incident indicates that the CCP’s internal <br />struggle is being exposed to the public.</p>
<p>HK Student Leader Will Speak at UN Summit on<br />Human Rights and Democracy</p>
<p>Student leaders of the Occupy Central movement,<br />Alex Chow and Lester Shum will attend the United Nations <br />Geneva Summit for Human Rights and Democracy in late <br />February as speakers. <br />The official website has put their photos on the home page, <br />and announced that both of them will speak at the summit, <br />along with other human rights and democratic activists <br />from Iran, Russia, North Korea, Cuba and Venezuela. </p>
