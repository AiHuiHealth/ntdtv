<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】大陆网友自爆换肾经历：14人同时换</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JvP6D"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tjvrql262/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/tjvrql262/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年05月17日讯】日前，一位大陆网友向本台爆料13年前他做肾移植的情况。当时在医院内有14位病人同时换肾，而且医生向他透露，供体的身体好。他怀疑肾脏是从法轮功学员身体上<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/活摘.md#1">活摘</a>的。请看以下的报导。</p>
<p>日前，一位不愿意透露姓名的大陆网友向《新唐人》爆料了自己13年前，在广州换肾的经历。</p>
<p>大陆网友：“我是01年得尿毒症的，我01年发现的，两个月之后就换了（肾脏）。运气好，我排队就半个小时，血型单传真过去，半个小时他就回话明天来住院。”</p>
<p>据这名网友透露，他当时是在广州做的手术，总共花费8万元。当时为他做手术的医生是一名军医，这位医生曾向他透露过一些信息。</p>
<p>大陆网友：“因为我那个女朋友是护士，他就说我那个供体是一个28岁的年轻人，贵州的，身体比较好，他只说了这么三个特点，其他他就没有说，至于是干什么的没说，姓名更加不会说。”</p>
<p>而且，这名网友透露，在做手术当天，与他一起做手术的还有其他13名尿毒症患者。</p>
<p>大陆网友：“我那天做手术的时候总共我们14个人一起做手术，7个是台湾的，7个是国内的。我们在手术室等著，肾源是飞机空运过来的，去飞机场接，我们在手术室能听到救护车来了，我们就知道肾源到了，医生就把我们肚子切开，就开始做手术。”</p>
<p>据了解，在做<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/器官移植.md#1">器官移植</a>手术前有一个复杂的手续，一是要做配型﹔二是要花长时间找到适合的供体，但是这位网友怎么这么“幸运”，半个小时之内就做完了配型？而且同一时间有14人一起做手术，当天怎么这么巧有这些供体？</p>
<p>据美国卫生部报告（www.organdonor.gov），在美国等待肾移植平均需要1121天，肝移植796天，心脏移植231天。在2000年前的中国移植界也是这样。</p>
<p>2010年3月，广东《南方周末》记者在《器官捐献迷宫》这篇报导中，采访广州“中山大学附属第一医院”副院长何晓顺时得知，“2000年是中国<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/器官移植.md#1">器官移植</a>的分水岭。2000年全国的肝移植比1999年翻了10倍，2005年又翻了3倍。”而中共官方公布的数据，2000年只比1999年翻了1倍多。</p>
<p>美国中文杂志《新纪元周刊》报导，根据中共官方公布的每年移植数量，中国已成为美国之后的第二大移植大国。据“中华医学会器官移植学会”主任委员陈实介绍，截至2005年底，中国已累计开展器官移植8万5千多例，其中肾移植7万4千多例，肝移植上万例，心脏移植4千多例。特别是2002年以来，每年开展的器官移植手术超过1万例，2005年达到了创记录的1万2千多例。</p>
<p>这么多的器官供体到底从哪里来的？中共当局曾经6次改口，声称大多数器官来自于死刑犯。然而根据国际人权组织调查，中国每年公布的死刑犯在二千人左右，即使全部用上，也无法解答大陆器官移植的数量。</p>
<p>因此有国际医学专家分析认为，大陆一定存在庞大的“地下人体器官库”，甚至“活体器官库”。</p>
<p>2006年7月，由加拿大前亚太司司长、资深国会议员大卫?乔高（David Kilgour）、和国际人权律师大卫?麦塔斯（David Matas）组成的独立调查组，向国际社会公布了“中国活体摘取法轮功学员器官指控的报告”。报告从12个方面汇集了调查的起因、方法、证据、反证、可信度、结论及建议等。最后得出结论，这项指控是真实的。报告说“这是这个星球上从未发生过的邪恶”。</p>
<p>而且，2011年12月8号，美国知名调查记者伊桑葛特曼(Ethan Gutmann)在美国国会作证说：“2008年北京奥运前，约有6万5千位法轮功学员，在心脏还在跳动的情况下，被<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/活摘.md#1">活摘</a>了器官。”</p>
<p>去年8月27号，美国《大纪元》新闻集团独家获取一份录音文件，据说是2006年9月13号，时任商务部长的薄熙来，随同时任中共总理温家宝，访问德国汉堡时的一段录音，他亲口承认了“江泽民下达了活摘法轮功学员器官的命令”。</p>
<p>1999年4月，当时的中共党魁江泽民，在政治局会议发言中明确提出，对法轮功学员要“名誉上搞臭，经济上搞垮，肉体上消灭”。此后，在江泽民的默许和纵容下，中国大陆发生大规模的活体摘取法轮功学员以及死刑犯器官的事例。被关进劳教营的法轮功修炼人被当作活体器官库，而且直到现在也一直在发生著。</p>
<p>采访编辑/田净 后制/孙宁</p>
<p>A Chinese Witness: 14 People Had Their Kidney Changed At Same Time</p>
<p>Recently, a Chinese netizen who had his kidney<br />transplanted 13 years ago contacted NTD Television.<br />He said that 14 kidney patients had their kidney changed<br />at the same time with him in the hospital.<br />A doctor told him that the kidney donor was very healthy.</p>
<p>This patient suspected that the kidney was from live organ<br />harvesting of a Falun Gong practitioner.<br />Let&#8217;s take a look.</p>
<p>This patient didn&#8217;t give his name for security reasons, he told<br />NTD about his experiences in Guangzhou.</p>
<p>A Chinese netizen: &#8220;I affected uremia in 2001,<br />I noticed this problem in 2001.<br />Two months later I had my kidney changed.<br />I was lucky I only waited for 30 minutes after I stood in a queue,<br />they faxed out my blood type, half an hour later,<br />they told me to stay in the hospital next day."</p>
<p>This netizen reveals that he had his operation done<br />in Guangzhou, it cost him 80,000 yuan.<br />It was a military doctor did the operation for him,<br />This military doctor gave him some information.</p>
<p>Chinese netizen: &#8220;Because my girlfriend is a nurse.<br />The military doctor told me that the donor was 28 years old<br />and was healthy, the donor was from Guizhou Province.<br />He only told me the three issues, nothing else,<br />They didn&#8217;t say what the donor does, let alone the name."</p>
<p>This netizen reveals that there were other 13 uremia<br />patients had operations on the same day.</p>
<p>Chinese netizen: &#8220;On that day, 13 other people<br />had the kidney transplanted.<br />Seven were Taiwanese, seven<br />were mainland Chinese including me.<br />We were waiting in the operating room, the kidneys were<br />delivered by airplane, they collected it from an airport.<br />In the operating room, I could hear the ambulance come.<br />We knew the kidneys had arrived.<br />Doctors opened my abdomen and started the operation."</p>
<p>Sources say that there was a complicated process<br />before the transplants.<br />Firstly was to find matched blood type,<br />secondly was to find matched donor.<br />However, how was this netizen so lucky, trhat it only took a half<br />hour to find a matched kidney?<br />Furthermore, there were 14 people had the kidney<br />transplants on the same day.<br />How could they be so lucky to have these donors?</p>
<p>According to the US health ministry&#8217;s report on<br />organdonor.gov website,<br />a kidney patient in the US needs to wait for<br />about 1,121 days to have kidney transplanted.<br />a liver patient needs to wait for 796 days,<br />a heart patient needs 231 days.<br />It was a similar waiting time before 2000 in China.</p>
<p>In March 2010, Guangdong journalist of Southern Weekly<br />published an article titled &#8220;Puzzle of Organs Donors".<br />The reporter interviewed He Xiaoshun, Deputy Director of<br />the First Affiliated Hospital of Zhongshan Medical University.<br />&#8220;2000 is the turning point for China&#8217;s organ transplantation.<br />Liver transplantation increased<br />10 times more than in 1999.<br />In 2005, it increased 3 times", said He Xiaoshun.</p>
<p>However, China official statistics said that 2000 only increased<br />one time more than in 1999.</p>
<p>US-based Chinese magazine Epoch Weekly reported that<br />according to China&#8217;s official statistics on annual organ transplantation numbers,<br />China was behind the US, becoming the world&#8217;s<br />second transplantation country.<br />Chen Shi, member of Chinese Society of Organ Transplantation<br />said that at the end of 2005, more than 85,000 organ transplants were done.<br />Amongst them, there were 74,000 kidney transplants,<br />over dozens of thousands of liver transplants,<br />more than 4,000 heart transplants.</p>
<p>Especially from 2002, every year, there were more than<br />10,000 organ transplants done.<br />In 2005, it broke the record that reached<br />more than 12,000 organ transplants.</p>
<p>Where do so many organs come from?</p>
<p>The Chinese regime announced six times about<br />donors&#8217; sources but each time was different.<br />It claimed that majority of the donors were<br />executed prisoners.<br />However, according to international human rights<br />organization&#8217;s investigation,<br />about 2,000 executed prisoners were announced each year.</p>
<p>Even if they are all donors, it is difficult to explain the large<br />number of organ transplants taking place in China.</p>
<p>Some international medical experts analyze that in China,<br />underground human organ stores likely exist.<br />People have even been harvested of their organs while alive.</p>
<p>In July 2006, David Kilgour, former Canadian Secretary of<br />State (Asia-Pacific), member of parliament,<br />and David Matas, international human rights lawyer,<br />formed an independent investigation group,<br />and published a report about allegations of live organ harvesting<br />from Falun Gong practitioners in China.<br />The report brings 12 aspects of the investigation together.</p>
<p>It includes the causes, methods, proof, disproof, credibility,<br />conclusion and suggestion etc.<br />The final conclusion is that the allegations are true.<br />The report says that &#8220;this is indeed<br />&#8216;a form of evil yet to be seen on this planet.'"</p>
<p>On Dec. 8, 2011, Ethan Gutmann, a US investigative<br />journalist, testified to the US Congress.<br />He said that before the Beijing Olympics in 2008, there were<br />about 65,000 Falun Gong practitioners being live<br />harvested of their organs, with their hearts still beating.</p>
<p>On August 27, 2013, US-based Epoch Times group<br />exclusively obtained an audio file.<br />The report said that on Sept. 13, 2006, Bo Xilai was then<br />China&#8217;s Minister of Commerce,<br />he visited Hamburg in Germany with then Premier Wen Jiabao,<br />he admitted in the audio recording, it was Jiang Zemin issued<br />an order to live harvest Falun Gong practitioners&#8217; organs.</p>
<p>In April 1999, Jiang Zemin was the leader<br />of the Chinese Communist Party,<br />he clearly stated in a meeting of the politburo standing<br />committee how to deal with Falun Gong.<br />He said “defaming their reputation, bankrupting them<br />financially, destroy them physically.”<br />Since then, under Jiang&#8217;s acquiescence and connivance,<br />many Falun Gong practitioners and executed prisoners<br />were organ harvested when they were still alive.</p>
<p>The large source of live organs were Falun Gong practitioners<br />who were illegally sent to labor camps.<br />It is still happening today.</p>
