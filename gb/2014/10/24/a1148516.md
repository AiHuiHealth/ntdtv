<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】梁光烈证实中共中央军委参与活摘</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JvP6G"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tjvrql262/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/tjvrql262/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年10月24日讯】<b><ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/梁光烈.md#1">梁光烈</a>证实<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a><ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中央军委.md#1">中央军委</a>参与活摘</b></p>
<p>就在<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a>四中全会召开的同时，海外“追查迫害法轮功国际组织（简称追查国际）”，10月22号发布了一份最新调查报告。</p>
<p>报告说，“追查国际”就中共活摘法轮功学员器官的罪行，先后对中共原国防部长<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/梁光烈.md#1">梁光烈</a>、原政治局常委曾庆红和原<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中央军委.md#1">中央军委</a>副主席郭伯雄，进行了电话调查和取证。</p>
<p>在追查国际公布的电话录音中，前中共国防部长梁光烈亲口承认，中共中央军委曾开会讨论军队关押法轮功学员，以及军队医院活摘法轮功学员器官事宜，并向追查国际调查员表示，应向中共军方总后勤部了解具体情况。</p>
<p>而中共前中央军委副主席郭伯雄，则对调查员强调要“通过保密电话”详谈﹔前中共国家副主席、政治局常委曾庆红也强调需要“通过正常渠道”了解内情。</p>
<p><b>中共四中全会公告 未提周永康</b></p>
<p>中共十八届四中全会10月23号闭幕，会议发布的《中共中央关于全面推进依法治国若干重大问题的决定》中，没有提到中共前政治局常委周永康案的内容，令外界大感意外。在此之前，中外传媒普遍认为，四中全会上，将公布周永康案的处理决定，宣布开除他的党籍。</p>
<p>不过，英国《路透社》随后有报导说，周永康案很可能将在10月25号召开的中纪委全会上宣布结果。</p>
<p><b>评：中共四中全会 法制全面倒退</b></p>
<p>而对于中共当局高调宣传的“全面推进依法治国”，会议公告给出的内容，也让各界大失所望。</p>
<p>六四参与者、原清华大学学生李恒青在网上发表评论说，“中共18届四中全会是中国法制建设的全面倒退”。</p>
<p>他说，原以为中共只是原地踏步，了无新意罢了。没想到，中共的四中全会公告，全面推翻了过去曾经提到的“将权力关进笼子”，“共产党也要遵守宪法”等道貌岸然的说辞，一改羞羞答答的姿态，直截了当地要求，中共要统治一切。</p>
<p><b>香港狮子山现巨幅标语 撑占领</b></p>
<p>香港学生和市民发起的争取真普选占领行动，已经踏入第26天，10月23号，一批署名“香港蜘蛛仔”的登山爱好者，在像征香港精神的狮子山上，挂起了一幅长28米宽6米，写有“我要真普选”的巨型标语，声援占领行动。这个标语在乐富、黄大仙地区都可以观看到。</p>
<p>这群登山爱好者还在youtube上发表名为“狮子山为基层撑起雨伞”的视频，呼吁不同市民以自己的方式让运动遍地开花，共同要求香港政府认真回应香港市民的诉求，无畏无惧地向中共争取真普选。</p>
<p>编辑/周玉林</p>
<p><b>Liang Guanglie Confirmed &#8211; CCP Central Military Participate <br />Live Organ Harvesting </b></p>
<p>At the same time as the CCP Fourth Plenary Session, World <br />Organization to Investigate the Persecution of Falun Gong <br />(WOIPFG) released a new survey report on October 22nd.</p>
<p>The report says, WOIPFG investigated crimes of the CCP former <br />Defense Minister Liang Guanglie, <br />former politburo&#8217;s standing committee Zeng Qinghong and Vice chairman of the Central Military Commission Guo Boxiong,<br />regarding the CCP’s organ harvesting from living Falun Gong <br />practitioners over the phone.</p>
<p>In a telephone recording, former Defense Minister Liang Guanglie personally admitted that:<br />the CPC Central Military Commission held discussions about <br />procuring detained Falun Gong practitioners for the military, <br />and the organ harvesting from living Falun Gong practitioners <br />in military hospitals.<br />He told the investigators to get details from the Army General <br />Logistics Department.</p>
<p>The former vice chairman of the CCP Central Military <br />Commission Guo Boxiong stressed for talks in detail through <br />a confidential telephone.</p>
<p>Former vice chairman and the CCP Politburo Standing <br />Committee Zeng Qinghong also stressed to get inside through <br />normal channel.</p>
<p><b>Despotic Fourth Plenary Gets Cold Feet on Zhou Yongkang </b></p>
<p>October 23, 18th CCP Fourth Plenary Session closed.</p>
<p>The Session announced the Central major decisions on their <br />despotic rule of law; but didn’t mention Zhou Yongkang <br />which surprised outsiders.</p>
<p>Prior to this, foreign media generally agreed that at the Fourth <br />Plenary Session, a decision would be announced in the<br />Zhou Yongkang case, and confirm his expulsion from the party.</p>
<p>However, British Reuters reported that the Zhou Yongkang <br />case is likely to be announce at The Central Discipline <br />Inspection Committee meeting on October 25th.</p>
<p><b>Comments: CCP Fourth Plenary Session is a Comprehensive <br />Legal Setback </b></p>
<p>The announcement of the meeting disappointed all walks of life<br />regarding the CCP’s high-profile publicity &#8220;to comprehensively <br />promote the rule of law".</p>
<p>June 4th participant, former Tsinghua student Li Hengqing <br />commented online that the 18th Fourth Plenary Session <br />is the full backset of China&#8217;s legal system."</p>
<p>He said, originally, he thought the CCP were just marking time, <br />nothing new. <br />Unexpectedly, the CCP Fourth Plenary announcement <br />comprehensively threw out the past view of: “locking the <br />powers in a cage" and “Communist Party must abide by the <br />Constitution".<br />They just callously decided to rule everything without any<br />reservations or checks.</p>
<p><b>A Huge Banner on Hong Kong Lion Mountain Supports <br />Occupation</b></p>
<p>It has been 26 days since the struggle begun for genuine universal suffrage by Hong Kong students and the public.<br />October 23, a group of climbers called, Hong Kong Spiders <br />hung a 28 meters long and 6 meters wide giant banner writing:<br />“I want genuine universal suffrage” in support of the occupation <br />on the Lion Mountain which symbolized the Hong Kong spirit.<br />You can see the slogan in Lok Fu, Wong Tai Sin region.</p>
<p>These climbers also published a video on youtube called:</p>
<p>&#8220;Lion Mountain hold up an umbrella for basic rights" <br />to call on people to make the movement everywhere in their <br />own way.</p>
<p>They want people to jointly request the Hong Kong Government <br />to seriously respond to the Hong Kong people’s appeal and <br />request universal suffrage from the CCP, fearlessly.</p>
