<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】中共前卫生部长亲证 江泽民下令活摘器官</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JvP6j"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tjvrql262/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/tjvrql262/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年10月03讯】日前追查迫害法轮功国际组织对外曝光了一段由中共军方前卫生部部长亲口证实，供认原中共国家主席<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/江泽民.md#1">江泽民</a>，下令活摘法轮功学员器官做移植的电话录音，那么这段录音也进一步证实了共同参与活摘罪行的还有中共司法系统、军队、武警以及地方医疗机构。追查国际表示，曝光的录音，还仅仅是调查结果的一小部分，更完整的罪证将很快交到国际法庭。</p>
<p>自2006年中共涉嫌活体摘除法轮功学员器官首次被曝光以来，当局一直以“证据不足”公开否认这一指控，并反指法轮功“捏造谣言”。外界也因“活摘”这一话题过分敏感和血腥，没有予以充分关注。</p>
<p>令人意外的是在接下来的几年中，将中共活摘黑幕“曝光”于天下，并引发国际社会关注的，竟然来自中共内部高官。</p>
<p>2012年中共前重庆市公安局长王立军出逃美领馆，引发了中共官场的巨大震荡。去年一份由前重庆市委书记薄熙来，亲口承认“<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/江泽民.md#1">江泽民</a>是活摘下令者”电话录音的曝光，犹如给了中共一记响亮的耳光。</p>
<p>9月30号，一份调查原中共解放军总后勤部卫生部部长白书忠的电话录音，被追查迫害法轮功国际组织曝光。录音中清楚表明，原中共国家主席江泽民，下令活摘法轮功学员器官的事实。</p>
<p>原中共解放军总后勤部卫生部部长白书忠：“喂？”<br />追查国际调查员：“喂，是原总后卫生部白书忠部长吗？”<br />白书忠：“啊，你是哪里呀？”<br />调查员：“……，我们有一些情况想向您了解一下啊！”<br />白书忠：“啊，你怎么着，你什么时间？有什么事？你说。”<br />调查员：“是这样，在您担任总后卫生部长的时候啊，摘取在押法轮功人员器官做器官移植手术这件事情是当时的总后部长王克布置的任务？还是军委直接下达的命令哪？”<br />白书忠：“当时是江主席啊，有一个批示。有一个批示的话就是说…说开展这些事情——就是器官移植，就是人员卖肾，做手术。这个应该说，就是开展肾移植的不单是军队、地方。”<br />调查员：“我们也得到了一些情报，就是说当时，联青部还负责关押了一批法轮功在押人员的器官供体，是不是？”<br />白书忠：“这个…这个当时的话…唉哟！我觉得，起码在我印象中…当时，是吧…因为当时江主席批示以后，反法轮功大家都做了很多工作。”<br />调查员：“你们和这些联勤一分部、二分部包括联勤四零分部，他们负责的军队医院有没有直接领导和被领导的关系？”<br />白书忠：“我们这几个军医大学——就是能掌控的、咱们总后直属单位几个军医大学反复要求，因为那时江很注意这个问题，很重视这个问题…都有批示的。”</p>
<p>追查迫害法轮功国际组织负责人、哈佛大学医学博士汪志远表示，之所以要调查白书忠，是因为他是中共军方卫生医疗系统，执行活摘器官的最高医疗机构的负责人，对活摘命令下达的来源，以及整个部署和执行过程，是最主要的知情人和执行者。</p>
<p>汪志远：“因为他任职，是98年到04年，正好是活摘器官在中国大陆执行最严重的时期，而且这次执行的机构主要是以军队武警为主体，当然还有地方的相关医疗机构参与。”</p>
<p>追查国际确认，电话录音中的中共官员，就是白书忠本人无疑。</p>
<p>汪志远：“跟他通话进行调查之前，我们进行了全齐的调查。对他的身份、他的联系方式、电话号码、他的办公地点和他办公的时间都进行了核整。并且在通话的时候，是通过总机、还有接话转线啊等等，这个过程中都可以确认他的身份。”</p>
<p>汪志远还指出，这次调查证据的意义在于，进一步明确和证实了活体摘除法轮功学员器官，在中国大陆确实存在，而且是由时任中共总书记江泽民为首的中共当局，利用整个国家机器，进行的一场涉及到全国的大屠杀。追查国际将会对所有涉嫌其中的人，追查到底！</p>
<p>采访/编辑/张天宇 后制/李智远</p>
<p>Former Military Health Minister Says Jiang Zemin Is The Culprit Of Organ Harvesting</p>
<p>Former Health Minister of the General Logistics Department<br />of the People&#8217;s Liberation Army (PLA) personally confirmed<br />that former Chinese President Jiang Zemin ordered<br />live organ harvesting from Falun Gong practitioners. <br />These organs were used for transplants according <br />to telephone records recently released <br />by World Organization to Investigate<br />the Persecution of Falun Gong (WOIPFG).<br />The telephone record also confirmed that the organ <br />harvesting crimes have been jointly committed <br />by the judicial system, the military, armed police and local <br />medical institutions of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP).<br />WOIPFG says the recordings expose only a small part<br />of the findings of the entire investigation process.<br />A more complete series of evidence will be soon<br />submitted to the International Tribunal.</p>
<p>Since 2006 when the CCP was exposedfor organ harvesting<br />from live Falun Gong practitioners for the first time, <br />the CCP Authorities have publicly denied the allegations <br />with the excuse of &#8220;insufficient evidence."<br />They turned their backs and attacked innocent Falun Gong,<br />saying the accusations were fabricated rumors.<br />The outside world also did not pay full attention<br />to this event due to the bloodiness and sensitive nature <br />of the subject.</p>
<p>Surprisingly, in the following few yearshigh-ranking CCP <br />officials revealed the CCP&#8217;s covert living organ harvesting.<br />They exposing it to the world, which sparked <br />international concern.</p>
<p>In 2012, the former Director of Chongqing Municipal<br />Public Security Bureau Wang Lijun fled into<br />the United States Consulate in Chengdu City,<br />triggering a huge shock in the CCP officialdom.<br />Last year, one telephone recording exposed that former <br />Chongqing CCP Head Bo Xilai himself admitted that <br />&#8220;Jiang Zemin ordered the execution of live organ <br />harvesting", a strong slap in the CCP&#8217;s face.</p>
<p>On Sept 30, a portion of a telephone recording regarding<br />former Health Minister of the PLA General Logistics<br />Department Bai Shuzhong was released by WOIPFG.</p>
<p>The recordings clearly indicates that former Chinese<br />President Jiang Zemin ordered organ harvesting <br />from live Falun Gong practitioners.</p>
<p>Former Health Minister of PLA General Logistics Department<br />Bai Shuzhong: &#8220;Hello?" </p>
<p>WOIPFG Investigator: &#8220;Hey, are you former<br />Health Minister of the General Logistics Department<br />Bai Shuzhong?"</p>
<p>Bai Shuzhong: &#8220;Ah, where are you from?" </p>
<p>Investigator: &#8220;&#8230;&#8230; we need to understand<br />some cases from you!"</p>
<p>Bai Shuzhong: &#8220;Ah, you how, what time,<br />what sort of thing? Please speak."</p>
<p>Investigator: &#8220;So, when you served as the Health Minister<br />of the General Logistics Department, was the execution<br />of organ harvesting from Falun Gong practitioners<br />under custody as organ transplants ordered<br />by the Head of the General Logistics Department Wang Ke<br />or by the Central Military Commission of the CCP?"</p>
<p>Bai Shuzhong: &#8220;At that time President Jiang<br />gave a written instruction. <br />The instruction required to&#8230;to carry out these things – organ <br />transplantation, selling kidneys and performing operations.<br />It should be said that to carrying out kidney transplants<br />was not only undertaken by the military,<br />but also by civil institutions."</p>
<p>Investigator: &#8220;We also have some intelligence information. </p>
<p>At that time, the United Logistics Department was also <br />responsible for a number of imprisoned Falun Gong<br />detainees as organ donors. Is this right?"</p>
<p>Bai Shuzhong: &#8220;This &#8230; this is that time&#8217;s case &#8230; I think,<br />at least in my impression &#8230; that time&#8230;<br />after the President Jiang issued out the instructions,<br />we have done a lot of work on anti-Falun Gong!"</p>
<p>Investigator: &#8220;Do you have the direct leadership<br />and led relationship with the First and the Second<br />United Logistics Department as well as their 40 Branch,<br />and their affiliated military hospitals?"</p>
<p>Bai Shuzhong: &#8220;A few our Military Medical University<br />– which are under our control and directly under<br />the administration of the General Logistics Department,<br />had been repeated with the requests.<br />Because then Jiang paid much attention to the issue,<br />emphasized the issue&#8230;he had instructions in this issue."</p>
<p>WOIPFG Head Wang Zhiyuan is a medical PhD with a degree<br />from Harvard University. <br />He said the reason for investigating Bai Shuzhong is that <br />he served in the highest position in charge of implementing <br />organ harvesting among the Chinese military health care <br />system, and he is the most important insider and executive.<br />He knew the order source, deployment<br />and implementation process of live organ harvesting.</p>
<p>Wang Zhiyuan: &#8220;His tenure was from 1998 to 2004, which <br />was just the most serious period of live organ harvesting<br />occurrences in China. </p>
<p>The execution of the main body covers the armed forces <br />and military medical institutions, and of course some <br />local civil medical institutions."</p>
<p>WOIPFG confirmed that the CCP official on the telephone<br />recording is Bai Shuzhong himself without doubt.</p>
<p>Wang Zhiyuan: &#8220;Prior to this call with the investigation,<br />we carried out a full investigation together with his identity, <br />his contact information, phone number,<br />his office location and his office time.<br />During the phoning process, through the switchboard,<br />telephone operator etc., his identity can be confirmed."</p>
<p>Wang Zhiyuan also noted that the importance of the survey <br />evidence had further clarified and confirmed that the live <br />organ harvesting from Falun Gong practitioners does exist <br />in China, and is a nationwide massacre chaired<br />by that time&#8217;s CCP General Secretary Jiang Zemin<br />through the entire state apparatus.<br />WOIPFG will thoroughly investigate<br />all suspected persons until the end!</p>
