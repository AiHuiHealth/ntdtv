<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】升级行动未达目标 双学承认失败</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年12月02日讯】<b>升级行动未达目标 双学承认失败 </b></p>
<p>香港学界11月30号升级占领行动，发动包围政府总部后，遭到警察铁腕清场，多名示威者被殴打致头破血流，另有52人被捕。12月1号，“香港专上学生联会”秘书长周永康，承认升级行动失败，向受伤战友躹躬道歉。</p>
<p>据香港多家媒体报导，周永康表示，升级行动没有达到瘫痪政府总部的目标，有不足之处，愿意为此致歉。</p>
<p>周永康说，围堵政府总部是要向港府施压，希望当局回应政改诉求，虽然大部分示威者都恪守非暴力原则，但还是遭到警方粗暴对待。</p>
<p>“学民思潮”召集人<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/黄之锋.md#1">黄之锋</a>也说，学生有不足之处，但警方的失控情况，实在超出每一个人的意料。</p>
<p><b><ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/黄之锋.md#1">黄之锋</a>与2学民成员无限期<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/绝食.md#1">绝食</a></b></p>
<p>1号晚间，黄之锋与另外2名“学民思潮”女成员在金钟大台上宣布，从即日起，在金钟添美道无限期<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/绝食.md#1">绝食</a>，要求港府与“学民”就政改公开对话。</p>
<p>黄之锋说，绝食并非因为升级行动失败用来挽回公众形象，只是希望能与政府对话，重返谈判桌，绝食过程有医疗义工监察，期间只喝清水。</p>
<p>另外2名绝食的女学生，分别是香港浸会大学传理学院一年级生卢彦慧和中六学生黄子悦。</p>
<p>他们都为连月来很少陪伴父母，向家人道歉。</p>
<p><b> 藏族艺人因歌词涉嫌政治内容获刑</b></p>
<p>藏族歌手格桑亚培和音乐制片人白玛仁增，因演唱的歌曲内容被指控涉嫌政治问题，11月27号分别被中共当局判刑4年和2年。</p>
<p>据“西藏之声”网站报导，格桑亚培去年7月被抓，已经被监禁了16个月。被中共当局指控涉嫌政治问题的歌曲，名叫“藏人们”，歌词大意是鼓励藏人学藏文、说藏语、团结一致，为藏人的未来寻找理想。</p>
<p>“法新社”报导说，不少藏人指控中共政府通过加强在藏区的控制，试图要根除藏族的文化。 </p>
<p>编辑/周玉林</p>
<p><b>Upgrade Action Has to Admit Defeat </b></p>
<p>On Nov. 30, Hong Kong students launched an activity to<br />surround the government headquarters.<br />It is to upgrade the occupation after the police <br />clearing actions.<br />Several demonstrators were beaten badly and 52 arrested. </p>
<p>On Dec. 1, Hong Kong Federation of Students (HKFS) <br />Secretary-General Zhou Yongkang apologized to the injured <br />occupants by bowing and admitted the upgrade failed.</p>
<p>According to several media reports in Hong Kong, Zhou <br />Yongkang is willing to apologize because the campaign had shortcomings and did not reach the target of paralyzing the <br />government headquarters.</p>
<p>Zhou said the containment was to push the authorities to <br />respond to the political reform demand.<br />Although most protesters obeyed the non-violence principle, <br />they still received maltreatment from the police.</p>
<p>Scholarism convener Huang Zhifeng also said that students <br />also have shortcomings; <br />However the police getting out of control is beyond <br />everyone&#8217;s expectation.</p>
<p><b>Huang Zhifeng And Two Members go on Indefinite Hunger <br />Strike </b></p>
<p>On Dec.1 Huang Zhifeng and two female Scholarism <br />members announced an indefinite hunger strike at Tim <br />Mei Avenue, Admiralty to request an open dialogue for <br />political reform between the government and Scholarism.</p>
<p>Huang Zhifeng said the hunger strike was not to restore <br />public image because of the failed upgrade action.<br />They just want to have a dialogue with the government and <br />return to the negotiating table.<br />There will be medical volunteers monitoring them during <br />the hunger strike and they will only drink water.</p>
<p>Two more female students on hunger strike are Lu Yanhui <br />from Hong Kong Baptist University first grade and sixth <br />grade student Huang Ziyue.</p>
<p>They apologized to their familes because they were rarely <br />with their parents recently.</p>
<p><b>Tibetan Artists Received Jail Terms For Political <br />Sounding Lyrics </b></p>
<p>On Nov. 27, Tibetan singer Kalsang Yarphel and music <br />producer Baima Renzeng were sentenced to four years <br />and two years imprisonment by the authorities due to <br />political issues.</p>
<p>According to Tibet Voice website report, Kalsang Yarphel has <br />been imprisoned for 16 months since arrested in July 2013.<br />The alleged political songs are called &#8220;Tibetan People".</p>
<p>The lyrics are to encourage Tibetans to learn Tibetan <br />language and unite to find the future of the Tibetan people.</p>
<p>AFP reported that many Tibetans accused the Chinese regime<br />of trying eradicate their culture by strengthening control in <br />the Tibetan areas.</p>
