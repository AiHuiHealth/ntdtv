<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】鲁炜考察脸书 大陆网友吐槽</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年12月09日讯】 <b> 鲁炜考察<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/脸书.md#1">脸书</a> 大陆网友吐槽</b></p>
<p>大陆长期封杀网路社交平台<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/脸书.md#1">脸书</a>（Facebook），但是，中共互联网信息办主任鲁炜，日前却亲自造访脸书总部。大陆媒体还高调报导说，鲁炜在脸书创办人马克?扎克伯格座位上，发现了一本英文版的习近平著作。</p>
<p>由于鲁炜曾在被媒体问到脸书为何遭中共封锁时，回答说，中国只欢迎真朋友，因此，他亲访脸书的消息一发出，立刻引起大陆网友的吐槽，而扎克伯格的举动，更是招来一片调侃。</p>
<p>有人说，鲁炜这样的人，怎么好意思去人家扎克伯格的办公室的，脸皮太厚。也有人说，扎克伯格能做出这样的事情，背后一定有人指点，否则他就是中共预备党员了。</p>
<p>但是，更多的人却担心，脸书为打入中国市场，可能会出卖用户。</p>
<p><b> 撑学生港民发起接力绝食</b></p>
<p>香港“<ahref="https://github.com/eirdkz3742/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/占领运动.md#1">占领运动</a>”8号进入第72天，示威者最集中的金钟占领区本周面临清场，“学民思潮”要求与港府对话而发起的绝食行动，也踏入了第6天，目前还有一人仍在坚持，而港府依然没有重启对话的迹象。</p>
<p>12月7号，十多名香港市民也联合发起28小时接力绝食行动，绝食营帐设在学民思潮绝食区对面，目前已经有近百人在网上响应这个行动。</p>
<p>据《自由亚洲电台》报导，参与绝食的市民表示，虽然明知绝食未必能迫使政府重启政改，但是，希望通过行动，让学生感受到他们并非孤军作战。</p>
<p><b> 伊力哈木七名学生被判刑</b></p>
<p>大陆知名维吾尔族学者伊力哈木的七名学生，12月8号被以分裂国家罪，分别被判处三至八年不等的徒刑。</p>
<p>伊力哈木的辩护律师之一刘晓原，当天在社交媒体推特上发文透露了这个消息。伊力哈木的另一名辩护律师李方平也对《美国之音》表示，他从警方那里也得到了相关消息。他说，那些对伊力哈木做出不利证词的学生，获得了较轻的判刑。</p>
<p>伊力哈木今年9月被以分裂国家罪判处无期徒刑，中共当局指责伊力哈木和他的这7名学生是犯罪集团，图谋把新疆分裂出去，但是，伊力哈木否认指控，国际舆论也谴责中共重判伊力哈木，是政治打压。</p>
<p>编辑/周玉林</p>
<p><b>Mainland Netizens Mocked Lu Wei&#8217;s Investigaion <br />of Facebook. </b></p>
<p>It has been a while since the Internet social platform Facebook <br />was banned in mainland.<br />However, State Internet Information Office director Lu Wei <br />wants to personally vist Facebook headquarters. <br />Mainland media wrote, Lu Wei saw an English version book <br />by Xi Jinping on Facebook founder Mark Zuckerberg&#8217;s seat.</p>
<p>Previously, Lu Wei said China only welcomes true friends to <br />respond to the Facebook block.<br />So his personal visit to Facebook triggered mockery from <br />mainland netizens.<br />And Zuckerberg&#8217;s movement also received teasing.</p>
<p>Some people said Lu Wei is very impudent to go to <br />Zuckerberg&#8217;s office.<br />Some also said there might be someone behind to help <br />Zuckerberg do such things; <br />Otherwise he was a probationary member of the Chinese<br />Communist Party (CCP).</p>
<p>However, more people worried that Facebook might betray <br />users to enter the China market.</p>
<p><b> Hong Kong Citizens Initiated Relay Hunger Strike to Support <br />Scholarism Student.</b></p>
<p>Dec. 8 is 72 days of the Hong Kong Occupy Central <br />movement.<br />The occupied Admiralty where most protesters are <br />concentrated is facing clearout this week.<br />The hunger strike launched by Scholarism entered 6 days<br />of requesting dialogue with the Hong Kong Government.<br />Currently there is only one student persisting and the <br />government hasn&#8217;t restarted a dialogue.</p>
<p>On Dec. 7, more than a dozen Hong Kong citizens also <br />launched a 28 hour relay hunger strike.<br />They put the hunger strike camps opposite the Scholarism <br />hunger strike area.<br />Currently nearly a hundred people responded to this action <br />through the internet.</p>
<p>Radio Free Asia reported, the hunger strike participants hope <br />to make students not feel alone.<br />Although they know a hunger strike won&#8217;t force the <br />government to restart the political reform.</p>
<p><b>Ilham&#8217;s Seven Students Sentenced. </b></p>
<p>On Dec. 8 renowned Uyghur scholar Ilham&#8217;s seven students <br />were sentenced.</p>
<p>They received from three to eight years imprisonment on <br />charges of crimes against the country.</p>
<p>One defense lawyer of Ilham, Liu Xiaoyuan posted the <br />information on Twitter on the same day.<br />Another defense lawyer Li Fangping also told the Voice of <br />America that he was given confirmation by the police. <br />He said those students who made unfavorable testimony <br />about Ilham received lighter sentences.</p>
<p>Ilham was sentenced to life imprisonment this September on <br />charges of crimes against the country.<br />The authorities accused Ilham and his seven students of being <br />a criminal group attempting to split Xinjiang.<br />Ilham denied the allegations and international opinion also <br />condemned political pressure to heavily sentence Ilham.</p>
