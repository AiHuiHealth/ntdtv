<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】阻中共强摘器官 逾215美议员联署</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JvPrQ"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tjvrql262/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/tjvrql262/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年11月07日讯】继欧洲议会去年12月通过决议案，要求<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a>立即停止<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/强摘器官.md#1">强摘器官</a>，去年6月发起的美国281号决议案，至今也已获得超过215位国会议员的联署。下面请看报导。</p>
<p>美国281号决议案已先后在众议院外交事务委员会亚太小组，以及众议院外交事务委员会，完成最后审议，目前正等待在众议院全体会议上，进行最后表决。</p>
<p>决议案明确要求<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a>立即停止针对法轮功学员，和其他良心犯的“<ahref="https://github.com/tjvrql262/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/强摘器官.md#1">强摘器官</a>”行为﹔并要求中共立即停止对法轮功已经持续15年的迫害，立即释放所有法轮功修炼者和其他良心犯。</p>
<p>众议院外委会亚太小组主席、资深国会议员史蒂夫?夏伯特（Steve Chabot）表示，281号决议案获得了亚太小组的全体支持。他说：“违背人们的意愿，进行器官移植的行径，应该受到谴责，这是完全不可接受的，令人毛骨悚然！”</p>
<p>夏伯特指出，强摘器官正在发生，问题是很多人并不知道。美国应该发挥作用，将中共的这一行径公之于众，让世界知道。</p>
<p>大陆维权律师唐吉田：“如果说美国国会对中国的人权问题，行使他们这个权力，并且督促美国行政系统能够真正的重视这个问题，对于改善中国的人权状况，从而促使中国官方能够接受国际上通行的一些标准，这不仅是大陆民众的一个好消息，同时这方面的努力客观上也有利于整个世界人权水平的提高。”</p>
<p>281号决议案发起人之一，众议院外交关系委员会前主席、佛州共和党籍资深众议员罗斯?雷婷恩(Ileana Ros-Lehtinen)表示，有大量证据显示，法轮功学员的器官被中共活摘，并被放在市场上贩卖。这种罪行是十分邪恶和可怕的行为，必须制止！</p>
<p>北京律师韩志广：“活摘器官都是犯罪，有人说，但是我没有亲眼见到，这个事实我都不敢相信的，如果真的存在这样的事情，确实是太残酷了，会遭到全人民共愤的，捉到的人必然要受到法律的严惩。”</p>
<p>“追查迫害法轮功国际组织”10月21号曝光了多个电话录音，其中原中共国防部长、中央军委委员梁光烈在电话中承认，中央军委曾开会讨论军队关押法轮功学员，以及军队医院活摘法轮功学员器官事宜。原中共军队总后勤部卫生部部长白书忠也承认，是前中共党魁江泽民直接下令活摘法轮功学员的器官。</p>
<p>美国众议院资深国会议员、外委会资深成员、政府监督与调查小组委员会主席德纳?罗拉巴克（Dana Rohrabacher）表示，这一活摘器官的野兽罪行是谋杀，这个决议案将有长久的效应，至少可以警醒美国人民，这一可怕的谋杀正在进行。</p>
<p>北京律师王雅军：“无论是国际公约，还是国内法，本身就是明令禁止的犯罪行为，活摘器官就是谋杀，如果有这方面的证据，就应该公开，但是在国内，这样的环境还是不太具备，但是不管怎么说，肯定是应该大家努力做的一件事情，要公开这些犯罪行为，呼吁大家共同的揭露，然后制裁相关的人员。”</p>
<p>曾为法轮功学员进行辩护，而被黑龙江建三江当局非法拘留15天的唐吉田律师，获释后曾告诉《新唐人》，他在被拘押期间遭受酷刑折磨，警察还威胁要将他“活体取肾、挖坑活埋”。</p>
<p>不过，由于活摘器官太过于血腥残暴，唐律师至今仍不敢相信。</p>
<p>唐吉田：“从网上看到一些报导，如果这种事情属实，那么就不是一般的刑事犯罪，那是一种系统的反人类的犯罪，无论是具体实施的人，还是指挥决策的人，除了接受国内法的评判以外，也必要接受国际法的制约。”</p>
<p>罗拉巴克议员指出，首先必须在国会通过281号决议案，然后将那些涉入罪行的人进行定位，将他们绳之以法，最后将这些人证摆在中共面前，要中共对此负责。</p>
<p>据了解，美国中期选举正在进行，更多联署281号决议案的国会议员名单，将在选举结束、国会复会后列入国会记录。</p>
<p>采访/秦雪 编辑/陈洁 后制/李智远</p>
<p>Over 215 House of Representatives Support H. Res. 281</p>
<p>Following the European Parliament&#8217;s resolution passed<br />last December, asking the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) <br />to immediately stop forced organ harvesting, the H. Res. 281 <br />by U.S. Congress reported in July has also won support <br />from 215 House of Representatives members. </p>
<p>Here&#8217;s the report.</p>
<p>The U.S. House Resolution 281 is gaining support from <br />a subcommittee of the House Committee on Foreign Affairs,<br />Asia and the Pacific, and is now awaiting a final vote.</p>
<p>The Resolution explicitly calls on the CCP to immediately end <br />the practice of organ harvesting from all prisoners; <br />demands an immediate end to the 15-year persecution of <br />the Falun Gong spiritual practice and the immediate release <br />of all Falun Gong practitioners and prisoners of conscience.</p>
<p>Chairman and Congressman Steve Chabot has indicated that<br />the H. Res. 281 has won the support of the entire committee <br />on Asia and the Pacific. </p>
<p>He says forcibly harvesting organs against a person&#8217;s will <br />is absolutely unacceptable and should be condemned.</p>
<p>Chabot says live organ harvesting is happening now<br />but many people are not aware of it. </p>
<p>He believes the United States should play a role <br />in exposing the CCP’s crimes to the world.</p>
<p>Tang Jitian, human rights lawyer: &#8220;If the US Congress uses <br />their power to urge the US administration to pay attention to<br />China’s human rights and prompt the CCP to adopt <br />international standards and improve the human rights, <br />it would be good news for both China and the world."</p>
<p>Sponsor of the resolution No. 281, Rep. Ileana Ros-Lehtinen <br />says that there is a large amount of evidence showing <br />the selling of forcibly harvested organs, and such ghoulish <br />and heinous crimes must be stopped.</p>
<p>Han Zhiguang Beijing lawyer: &#8220;Live organ harvesting is <br />a crime that some people question for not having seen it <br />with their own eyes; it’s hard for me to believe it really exists <br />because it’s too cruel and will cause wide-spread indignation; <br />the criminals will certainly be punished by law."</p>
<p>On Oct. 21, the World Organization to Investigate <br />the Persecution of Falun Gong (WOIPFG) revealed <br />a number of telephone recordings. </p>
<p>Included was a call to Liang Guanglie, former Minister for <br />China&#8217;s National Defense, in which he admitted that <br />the Central Military Commission had discussed the detention <br />of Falun Gong practitioners, and that the military hospitals <br />were engaging in live organ harvesting. </p>
<p>Another call was to Bai Shuzhong, ex- director of the Health <br />Department of the PLA General Logistics Department, <br />who admitted that former CCP leader, Jiang Zemin, had <br />directly ordered the organ harvesting from living Falun Gong <br />practitioners.</p>
<p>Congressman Dana Rohrabacher says the crimes of <br />organ harvesting are murderous. </p>
<p>The Resolution will have a lasting effect in alerting <br />Americans that such terrible murder is in progress.</p>
<p>Wang Yajun, Beijing lawyer: &#8220;Whether it&#8217;s an international <br />convention or national law, live organ harvesting is murder<br />and any evidence should be made public, but in China, <br />the environment does not allow it." </p>
<p>&#8220;But no matter what, everyone should try to expose this <br />criminal act, call on others to expose it, and put sanctions <br />on the people who are accountable."</p>
<p>Lawyer Tang Jitian was illegally detained for 15 days <br />for defending Falun Gong practitioners. </p>
<p>After his release, NTD was told that he had been tortured <br />during detention and the police had threatened to <br />take his kidney and bury him alive. </p>
<p>But Tang Jitain still finds the bloody cruelty of <br />live organ harvesting hard to believe.</p>
<p>Tang Jitian: &#8220;There are some reports on the Internet about<br />organ harvesting; if it’s true, it&#8217;s no ordinary criminal offense, <br />but a systematic crime against humanity." </p>
<p>&#8220;Those who conduct the acts or direct the acts will be <br />punished by laws and international sanctions."</p>
<p>Congressman Dana Rohrabacher has pointed out that <br />after the Congress passes the H. Res. 281, those involved <br />in the crimes will be located and brought to justice. </p>
<p>The criminals will be presented to the CCP <br />and the CCP should be held accountable the crimes.</p>
<p>It is understood that after the midterm elections, <br />more congressmen who endorsed the H. Res. 281 <br />will be included in the congressional record, <br />when parliament resumes.</p>
<p>Interview/QinXue Edit/ChenJie Post-Production/Li Zhiyuan</p>
